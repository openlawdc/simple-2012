The DC Code: § 6-1114 Severability.

Index → 6 Housing and Building Restrictions and Regulations. (Refs & Annos)

The sections of this subchapter are hereby declared to be severable. In the event that any section of this subchapter or portion thereof is held void or unenforceable for whatever reason, all remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
