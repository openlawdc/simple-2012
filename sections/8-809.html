The DC Code: § 8-809 Appeals.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)
a
The hearing examiner's decision may be appealed within 15 days of the issuance of the decision to the Board of Appeals and Review.
b
The parties may appeal a decision of the Board of Appeals and Review within 15 days of the issuance of the decision to the District of Columbia Court of Appeals.
