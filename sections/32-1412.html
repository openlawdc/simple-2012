§ 32-1412 "Secretary of Labor" defined.

As used or referred to in this subchapter the term "the Secretary of Labor" shall mean the administrator of that department or agency of the United States government authorized to administer the provisions of Public Law No. 308 (29 U.S.C. §§ 50-50b).
