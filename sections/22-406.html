The DC Code: § 22-406 Mayhem or maliciously disfiguring.

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)

Every person convicted of mayhem or of maliciously disfiguring another shall be imprisoned for not more than 10 years.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 22-506. 1973 Ed., § 22-506. DC CODE § 22-406 Current through December 11, 2012
