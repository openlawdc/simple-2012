The DC Code: § 34-406 Action pending March 4, 1913, unaffected by this subtitle.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)

This subtitle shall not affect actions or proceedings, civil or criminal, or quasi-criminal, pending on March 4, 1913, but the same may be prosecuted or defended as provided by preexisting law or regulation.
