§ 29-802.04 Signing and filing pursuant to judicial order.

a
If a person required by this chapter to sign a record or deliver a record to the Mayor for filing under this chapter does not do so, any other person that is aggrieved may petition the Superior Court to order:
1
The person to sign the record;
2
The person to deliver the record to the Mayor for filing; or
3
The Mayor to file the record unsigned.
b
If a petitioner under subsection (a) of this section is not the limited liability company or foreign limited liability company to which the record pertains, the petitioner shall make the company a party to the action.
