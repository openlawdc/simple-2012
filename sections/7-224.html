The DC Code: § 7-224 Matching birth and death certificates.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

The Registrar is authorized to match birth and death certificates in accordance with written regulations issued by the Mayor to prove beyond a reasonable doubt the fact of death, and to post the facts of death to the appropriate birth certificate. Copies issued from birth certificates marked deceased shall be similarly marked.
