§ 4-205.73 POWER--Application.

a
The Mayor may only consider TANF applicants or TANF recipients for consideration for POWER eligibility.
b
The Mayor may refer a TANF applicant or recipient for consideration of POWER eligibility at any time, including when a TANF applicant or recipient claims a medical incapacity exemption from work activities.
