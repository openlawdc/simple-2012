The DC Code: § 29-201.05 Status of filings.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A filing under this chapter signed by a domestic entity shall become part of the public organic document of the entity if the entity's organic law provides that similar filings under that law become part of the public organic document of the entity.
