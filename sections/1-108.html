The DC Code: § 1-108 Name of Uniontown changed to Anacostia.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

That portion of the District of Columbia prior to April 22, 1886, known and designated as Uniontown, shall be known and designated as Anacostia.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 1-108. 1973 Ed., § 1-108. DC CODE § 1-108 Current through December 11, 2012
