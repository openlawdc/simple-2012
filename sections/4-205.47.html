The DC Code: § 4-205.47 Nursing care standard.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
When a recipient is receiving nursing care in the home of a relative, the Mayor will apply the standard for room, board, and care in an intermediate care facility, based on the kind and extent of care required.
b
The rate for care in a foster home or for residential placement shall be the same as that for the lowest rate in an intermediate care facility.
c
The rates paid for intermediate care, foster home care, or residential placement shall be paid at 100% of the standard as the rates were not increased in the 1970 budget.
