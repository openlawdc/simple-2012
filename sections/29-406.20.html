The DC Code: § 29-406.20 Meetings.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
The board of directors may hold regular or special meetings in or outside of the District.
b
Unless the articles of incorporation or bylaws provide otherwise, the board of directors may permit any or all directors to participate in a regular or special meeting by, or conduct the meeting through the use of, any means of communication by which all directors participating may simultaneously hear each other during the meeting. A director participating in a meeting by this means shall be considered to be present in person at the meeting.
