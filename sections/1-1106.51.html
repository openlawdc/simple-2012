Legislative History of Laws Law 14-36, the "Campaign Finance Amendment Act of 2001", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 14-108, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 5, 2001, and June 26, 2001, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 12, 2001, it was assigned Act No. 14-98 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 14-36 became effective on October 13, 2001.
