The DC Code: § 28-4518 Relation to other law.

Index → 28 Commercial Instruments and Transactions. (Refs & Annos)

Nothing contained in this chapter shall be applicable to conduct or activity specifically regulated, permitted, or required by any regulatory body, agency, or commission acting under statutory authority of the District of Columbia or the United States.
