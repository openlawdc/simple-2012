The DC Code: § 38-408 Utilization of Business High School building.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

Upon completion of the Roosevelt (Business) High School the building now occupied by the Business High School shall be utilized for senior high and elementary school purposes.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 31-208. 1973 Ed., § 31-809. DC CODE § 38-408 Current through December 11, 2012
