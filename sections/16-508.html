§ 16-508 Attachment of real property.

An attachment is sufficiently levied on the lands and tenements of the defendant by:

"Levied on the following estate of the defendant, A B, to wit: (Here describe) this ___ day of ___. C D, Marshal."; and

1
mentioning and describing the property in an indorsement on the attachment, made by the officer to whom it is delivered for service, to the following effect:
2
serving a copy of the attachment, with the indorsement, and the notice required by section 16-502, on the person, if any, in possession of the property.
