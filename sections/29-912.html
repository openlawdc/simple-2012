The DC Code: § 29-912 Meetings; regular and special--Units of membership.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

The articles or bylaws may provide for the holding of meetings by units of the membership and may provide for a method of transmitting the votes there cast to the central meeting, or for a method of representation by the election of delegates to the central meeting, or for a combination of both of these methods.
