The DC Code: § 8-1703 Established.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)

There is established the Soil and Water Conservation District as a District of Columbia government agency.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 1-2803. Legislative History of Laws For legislative history of D.C. Law 4-143, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 8-1701. Editor's Notes Section 15 of D.C. Law 4-143 provided that the Soil and Water Conservation District, established by § 8-1703, shall terminate on January 1, 1987, unless it is subsequently reestablished by an act of the Council of the District of Columbia. Section 8-1721, reestablishing the Soil and Water Conservation District, was enacted July 25, 1987. DC CODE § 8-1703 Current through December 11, 2012
