§ 1-1061.12 Receipt of voted ballot.

a
A valid military-overseas ballot cast in accordance with § 1-1061.10 must be counted if it is delivered within 10 days after the election to the address that the Board has specified.
b
If, at the time of completing a military-overseas ballot and balloting materials, the voter has declared under penalty of perjury that the ballot was timely submitted, the ballot may not be rejected on the basis that it has a late postmark, an unreadable postmark, or no postmark.
