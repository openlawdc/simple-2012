§ 38-1806.08 Authorization of appropriations.

a
Authorization. --
1
Delta Council; access to state-of-the-art educational technology; and workforce preparation initiatives. -- There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out subsections (a), (b), and (d) of § 38-1806.04, $1,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1997, 1998, and 1999.
2
Deal Center. -- There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out § 38-1806.04(c), $2,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1997, 1998, and 1999.
3
Jobs for D.C. Graduates Program. -- There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out § 38-1806.07:
A
$2,000,000 for fiscal year 1997; and
B
$3,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1998 through 2001.
b
Availability. -- Amounts authorized to be appropriated under subsection (a) are authorized to remain available until expended.
