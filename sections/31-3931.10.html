The DC Code: § 31-3931.10 Name.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

A captive insurer shall not use or adopt a name that is the same as, deceptively similar to, or likely to be confused with or mistaken for any other insurer licensed in the District.
