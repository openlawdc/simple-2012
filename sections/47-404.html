§ 47-404 Account books of Collector.

a
It shall be the duty of the Collector to keep in his office account books, in which shall be entered:
1
The dates of payment of all taxes;
2
The amounts paid;
3
The names of the persons by whom payment has been made;
4
The years paid for;
5
The property paid on; and
6
The names of the persons to whom assessed.
b
His books shall at all times be open to the inspection of any officer who may be authorized by the Mayor of the District of Columbia to examine the same.
