The DC Code: § 29-1206.01 Beneficial interest.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
A beneficial interest in a statutory trust shall be freely transferable.
b
A beneficial interest in a statutory trust shall be personal property regardless of the nature of the property of the trust.
c
A beneficial interest in a statutory trust shall not be an interest in specific property of the statutory trust.
d
A beneficial owner shall not have a preemptive right to subscribe to any additional issue of beneficial interests or any other interest of a statutory trust.
