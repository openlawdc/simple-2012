The DC Code: § 21-308 Receipt for custodial property.

Index → 21 Fiduciary Relations and Persons with Mental Illness. (Refs & Annos)

A written acknowledgement of delivery by a custodian constitutes a sufficient receipt and discharge for custodial property transferred under this act.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 21-308. Legislative History of Laws For legislative history of D.C. Law 6-87, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 21-301. References in Text "This act", referred to at the end of the section, is the District of Columbia Uniform Transfers to Minors Act, D.C. Law 6-87 which is codified primarily as § 21-301 et seq.
