The DC Code: § 38-403 Use of Franklin School for office purposes.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

The Board of Education is authorized to use all necessary floor and room space in the Franklin School Building for office purposes.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 31-203. 1973 Ed., § 31-804. DC CODE § 38-403 Current through December 11, 2012
