The DC Code: § 49-806 Naval battalion not affected.

Index → 49 Military. (Refs & Annos)

Nothing contained in this subtitle shall be held to alter the status or organization of the naval battalion as now provided for by law.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 39-906. 1973 Ed., § 39-906. Act 18-387, May 5, 2010, 57 DCR 4325). Editor's Notes The reference to "this subtitle" in this section includes all sections of Subtitle I of Title 49 except §§ 49-217, 49-303, and 49-906.
