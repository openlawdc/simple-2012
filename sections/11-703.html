The DC Code: § 11-703 Judges; service; compensation.

Index → 11 Organization and Jurisdiction of the Courts. (Refs & Annos)
a
The chief judge and the judges of the court shall serve in accordance with Chapter 15 of this title.
b
Judges of the court shall be compensated at the rate prescribed by law for judges of the United States courts of appeals. The chief judge, while serving in that position, shall receive an additional $500 per annum.
