§ 10-1203.07 Collection and transfer of taxes to Washington Convention Center Fund.

a
Notwithstanding any other law, surtaxes and dedicated taxes shall be collected by the Mayor, pursuant to §§ 47-1807.02(a)(4), 47-1807.02a, 47-1808.03(a)(4), 47-1808.03a, 47-2002.02, 47-2002.03, 47- 2202.01, 47-2202.02, the provisions of which are incorporated by reference in this section, and transferred to the Washington Convention Center Fund for the purposes set forth in § 10-1202.08 until any of these provisions are repealed by legislation enacted after September 27, 1996.
b
This section shall apply as of September 27, 1996.
