§ 2-1212.22 Requirements.

a
As a condition of participating in the Program, an individual or legal entity operating a grocery store shall:
1
Accept SNAP benefits;
2
Apply to accept WIC benefits and accept WIC benefits if eligible;
3
Enter into a first source agreement; and
4
Sell fresh produce and healthy foods.
b
A grocery store shall agree in writing to the conditions set forth in subsection (a) of this section for a period of at least 5 years as a condition of participating in the Program; provided, that this requirement shall not be enforceable if the individual or legal entity ceases grocery store operations.
