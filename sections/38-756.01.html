§ 38-756.01 Rules.

a
The Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.
b
Each local education agency may advise the Mayor with respect to all proposed matters or rules issued pursuant to this chapter.
