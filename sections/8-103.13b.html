The DC Code: § 8-103.13b Fees, reimbursements, and costs.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall establish a schedule of fees for permits required by § 8-103.13a.
b
The Mayor may require reimbursement of costs for services including, inspections, sample collection, or document review pursuant to § 8-103.13a.
c
The Mayor may charge a fee for any permit issued pursuant to § 8-103.06.
