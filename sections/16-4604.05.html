§ 16-4604.05 Jurisdiction.

a
A petition under this subchapter may be filed only in a court that has jurisdiction to make a child-custody determination with respect to the child at issue under §§ 16-4601.01 to 16-4604.02.
b
A court of this state has temporary emergency jurisdiction under § 16- 4602.04 if the court finds a credible risk of abduction.
