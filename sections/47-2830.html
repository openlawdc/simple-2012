The DC Code: § 47-2830 Rental or leasing of motor vehicle without driver.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)
a
The owners or managers of establishments where automobiles or other motor vehicles are kept for rent or lease without a driver shall pay a license fee of $600 biennially for each such establishment; provided, that nothing in this section shall be so construed as to exempt such owners or managers from paying additional license taxes required by this chapter.
b
Any license issued pursuant to this section shall be issued as a Motor Vehicles Sales, Services and Repair endorsement to a basic business license under the basic business license system as set forth in subchapter I-A of this chapter.
