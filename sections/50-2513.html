The DC Code: § 50-2513 Tax exemption.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)
a
Bonds issued by the Authority and the interest thereon are exempt from District taxation except estate, inheritance, and gift taxes.
b
Real and personal property owned and used for exempt purposes by the Authority shall be exempt from District taxation.
