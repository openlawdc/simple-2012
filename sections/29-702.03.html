The DC Code: § 29-702.03 Statement of termination.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A dissolved limited partnership that has completed winding up may deliver to the Mayor for filing a statement of termination that states:

1
The name of the limited partnership;
2
The date of filing of its initial certificate of limited partnership; and
3
Any other information as determined by the general partners filing the statement or by a person appointed pursuant to § 29-708.03(c) or (d).
