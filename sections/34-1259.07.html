§ 34-1259.07 Rate and service regulation.

a
All proposed rates and service offerings for cable service shall be specified in an appendix to the franchise agreement.
b
A cable operator shall provide a notice of any planned change in rates or services to the Office at least 30 days before the change.
c
The Office may regulate cable services and rates in compliance with federal law and regulations; provided, that regulations promulgated by the Office to carry out this subsection shall be transmitted to the Council for a 60-day period of review.
