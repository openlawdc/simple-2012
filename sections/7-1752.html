The DC Code: § 7-1752 Prohibition on sale of cigarettes that are not fire-standard-compliant.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

No cigarette may be sold or offered for sale in the District of Columbia unless:

1
It has been tested in accordance with the test method specified in § 7- 1753(a)(2);
2
It meets the performance standard required by § 7-1753(a)(4);
3
The manufacturer has filed a written certification with the Mayor, or the Mayor's delegate, in accordance with § 7-1754; and
4
It has been marked in accordance with § 7-1754(b).
