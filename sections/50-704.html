§ 50-704 Commercial vehicle restrictions.

a
Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, the following vehicles are exempt from the purchase requirements contained in this chapter:
1
Any vehicle more than 26,000 pounds GVWR;
2
Emergency or law enforcement vehicles;
3
Nonroad vehicles, including farm and construction vehicles;
4
Vehicles in fleets operating in the covered area with fewer than 10 vehicles;
5
Vehicles in a covered fleet not capable of being centrally fueled;
6
Vehicles which are garaged under normal operations at a personal residence;
7
Vehicles leased or rented to the general public;
8
New car demonstration vehicles; and
9
Vehicles used for product demonstrations and tests.
b
The fact that one or more vehicles in a fleet is not centrally fueled does not exempt an entire fleet from the purchase requirements contained in this chapter.
