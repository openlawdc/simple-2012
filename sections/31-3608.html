The DC Code: § 31-3608 Monthly reports.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

Any time a long-term care benefit which is funded through a life insurance vehicle by the acceleration of the death benefit is in benefit payment status, a monthly report shall be provided to the policyholder. The monthly report shall include the following:

1
Any long-term care benefits paid out during the month;
2
An explanation of any changes in the policy (e.g., death benefits or cash values due to long-term care benefits being paid out); and
3
The amount of long-term care benefits existing or remaining.
