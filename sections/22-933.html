§ 22-933 Criminal Abuse of a Vulnerable Adult.

A person is guilty of criminal abuse of a vulnerable adult if that person intentionally or knowingly:

1
Inflicts or threatens to inflict physical pain or injury by hitting, slapping, kicking, pinching, biting, pulling hair or other corporal means;
2
Uses repeated or malicious oral or written statements that would be considered by a reasonable person to be harassing or threatening; or
3
Imposes unreasonable confinement or involuntary seclusion, including but not limited to, the forced separation from other persons against his or her will or the directions of any legal representative.
