The DC Code: § 23-703 Failure to appear.

Index → 23 Criminal Procedure. (Refs & Annos)

Any person released pursuant to section 23-702 who fails to appear as required shall be punished by a fine not exceeding $5,000 or imprisonment for not more than five years, or both.
