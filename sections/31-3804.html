The DC Code: § 31-3804 Applicability of chapter.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

The requirements of this chapter shall apply:

1
To all insurance policies and subscriber contracts delivered or issued for delivery in the District more than 120 days after October 20, 1979.
2
To all such insurance policies and subscriber contracts renewed, amended or reissued after 120 days following October 20, 1979;
3
To only children born more than 120 days after October 20, 1979;
4
To all individual subscriber contracts and group certificates issued or delivered in the District of Columbia by Group Hospitalization and Medical Services, Inc.;
5
To all for-profit as well as nonprofit indemnity type health insurers issuing or delivering individual indemnity type accident and sickness health insurance policies and group certificates in the District of Columbia; and
6
To health insurance certificates, except those described in § 31- 3802(2), that are delivered within the District of Columbia from group health insurance policies which are sold outside of the District of Columbia.
