§ 3-609 Liability of Commission members.

a
A member of the Commission shall not knowingly participate in any action of the Commission if such member, or the member's spouse or domestic partner, parent, grandparent, child, grandchild, brother, sister, uncle, aunt, cousin, nephew or niece, has a financial or business interest in the action.
b
A member shall not be liable in damages or court costs for any action of the Commission performed in good faith.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 2-609. 1973 Ed., § 2-1239. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 17-231, in subsec. (a), substituted "spouse or domestic partner" for "spouse".
