The DC Code: § 26-1308 Reports to Superintendent of Banking and Financial Institutions.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

All companies organized under this chapter, or which shall, under the provisions of this chapter, become entitled to transact the business of a trust company, shall report to the Superintendent of Banking and Financial Institutions pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 7 of this title.
