§ 42-3404.36 Assignment of District rights.

The Mayor may assign the opportunity to purchase provided under this subchapter to a person that:

1
Demonstrates the capacity to own and manage, either by itself or through a management agent, the housing accommodation and related facilities for the remaining useful life of the housing accommodation; and
2
Agrees to obligate itself and any successors in interest to maintain the affordability of the assisted housing development as required by § 42- 3404.33.
