The DC Code: § 5-331.02 Definitions.

Index → 5 Police, Firefighters, Medical Examiner, and Forensic Sciences. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term:

1
" First Amendment assembly" means a demonstration, rally, parade, march, picket line, or other similar gathering conducted for the purpose of persons expressing their political, social, or religious views.
2
"MPD" means the Metropolitan Police Department.
