The DC Code: § 40-403 Lien for rent, labor, or other charges.

Index → 40 Liens. (Refs & Annos)
a
The operator shall have a lien on all personal property stored within each leased space for rent, labor, or other charges, and for expenses reasonably incurred in its sale, as provided in this chapter.
b
The rental agreement shall contain a statement, in bold type, advising the occupant:
1
Of the existence of the lien; and
2
That property stored in the leased space may be sold to satisfy the lien if the occupant is in default.
