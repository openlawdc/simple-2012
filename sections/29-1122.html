The DC Code: § 29-1122 Distributions prohibited; compensation and other permitted payments.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) of this section , an unincorporated nonprofit association shall not pay dividends or make distributions to a member or manager.
b
An unincorporated nonprofit association may:
1
Pay reasonable compensation or reimburse reasonable expenses to a member or manager for services rendered;
2
Confer benefits on a member or manager in conformity with its nonprofit purposes;
3
Repurchase a membership and repay a capital contributions made by a member to the extent authorized by its governing principles; or
4
Make distributions of property to members upon winding up and termination to the extent permitted by § 29-1125.
