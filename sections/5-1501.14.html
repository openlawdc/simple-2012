§ 5-1501.14 Functions of the Stakeholder Council.

The Stakeholder Council shall:

1
Identify issues or concerns regarding:
A
The Department's delivery of forensic science services to agencies, including the timeliness of service; and
B
The general effectiveness of the Department in the furtherance of its agency mission; and
2
Advise the Mayor and the Council, as it considers necessary, on matters relating to the Department or forensic science.
