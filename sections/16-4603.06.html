The DC Code: § 16-4603.06 Enforcement of registered determination.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)
a
A court of the District may grant any relief normally available under the law of the District to enforce a registered child-custody determination made by a court of another state.
b
A court of the District shall recognize and enforce, but may not modify, except in accordance with subchapter II of this chapter, a registered child-custody determination of a court of another state.
