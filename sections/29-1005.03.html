§ 29-1005.03 No power as member to bind association.

A member, solely by reason of being a member, shall not act for or bind the limited cooperative association.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 503 of the Uniform Limited Cooperative Association Act .
