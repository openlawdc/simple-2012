The DC Code: § 2-1711 Annual report.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Office shall submit an annual report covering the preceding fiscal year to the Mayor by January 1st.
b
A copy of the annual report shall be sent to each member of the Council and placed in each branch of the public library.
