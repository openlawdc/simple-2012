§ 8-1702 Definitions.

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term:

1
"District of Columbia government agency" means any agency, department, unit, and instrumentality, corporate or otherwise, of the District of Columbia government.
2
"Renewable natural resources" means the land, the soil, the water, the vegetation, the trees, the fish, and the wildlife of the District of Columbia.
3
"Conservation" means conservation, improvement, maintenance, preservation, and protection of the renewable natural resources.
4
"Mayor" means the Mayor of the District of Columbia or the Mayor's designee.
5
"United States government agency" means any agency, department, unit, or instrumentality of the United States government.
