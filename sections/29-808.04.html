§ 29-808.04 Pleading.

In a derivative action under § 29-808.02, the complaint shall state with particularity:

1
The date and content of plaintiff's demand and the response to the demand by the managers or other members; or
2
If a demand has not been made, the reasons a demand under § 29-808.02(1) would be futile.
