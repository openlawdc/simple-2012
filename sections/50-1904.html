The DC Code: § 50-1904 Availability of test information.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)

Full information concerning the tests administered under this chapter shall be made available to the person from whom a specimen was obtained. Prior to administering the tests the police officer shall advise the operator of the motor vehicle about the requirements of this chapter.
