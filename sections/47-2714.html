The DC Code: § 47-2714 District of Columbia Village rates.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)
a
The per diem rate to be charged for skilled care patients at District of Columbia Village shall be $82.50.
b
The per diem rate to be charged for intermediate care patients at District of Columbia Village shall be $56.00.
