§ 21-2401.04 Communication between courts.

a
The Superior Court may communicate with a court in another state concerning a proceeding arising under this chapter. The court may allow the parties to participate in the communication. Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) of this section, the court shall make a record of the communication. The record may be limited to the fact that the communication occurred.
b
Courts may communicate concerning schedules, calendars, court records, and other administrative matters without making a record.
