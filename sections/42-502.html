The DC Code: § 42-502 Fee simple estates--Estates tail abolished.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

All estates of inheritance, including such as were formerly estates tail, shall be adjudged estates in fee simple.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-202. 1973 Ed., § 45-802. DC CODE § 42-502 Current through December 11, 2012
