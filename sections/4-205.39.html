The DC Code: § 4-205.39 Earned income.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)

Earned income includes income or resources in cash or in kind earned by an individual through the receipt of wages, salaries, commissions, or profit from activities in which he or she is self-employed.
