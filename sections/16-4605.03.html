The DC Code: § 16-4605.03 Transitional provision.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

A motion or other request for relief made in a child-custody proceeding or to enforce a child-custody determination which was commenced before April 27, 2001, is governed by the law in effect at the time the motion or other request was made.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 2001 Ed., § 16-4604.02. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 17-378 redesignated this section and substituted "April 27, 2001" for "the effective date of this chapter". D.C.
