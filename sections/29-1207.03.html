The DC Code: § 29-1207.03 Action on plan of merger by constituent statutory trust.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
A plan of merger shall be consented to by all trustees and all beneficial owners of a constituent statutory trust.
b
After a merger is approved, and at any time before a filing is made under § 29-1207.04, a constituent statutory trust may amend the plan or abandon the planned merger:
1
As provided in the plan; and
2
Except as prohibited by the plan, with the same consent as was required to approve the plan.
