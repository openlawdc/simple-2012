§ 8-231.07 Prohibition against retaliation.

a
A tenant may provide information to the Mayor concerning deteriorated paint or lead-based paint hazards within a property or elevated blood levels of a person at risk.
b
The provision of information in subsection (a) of this section shall be considered tenant rights.
