The DC Code: § 25-742 Solicitation of drinks prohibited.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)

The licensee under an on-premises retailer's license shall not:

1
Require, permit, suffer, encourage, or induce an entertainer or employee to solicit in the licensed establishment the purchase by a patron of any drink, whether alcoholic or non-alcoholic, or money with which to purchase the drink, for that entertainer or employee, or for any other person other than the patron and guests of the patron; or
2
Pay to the licensee's agent or manager, or any other person frequenting the licensed establishment, a commission or any other compensation to solicit for herself, himself, or for others, the purchase by the patron of any drink, whether alcoholic or non-alcoholic.
