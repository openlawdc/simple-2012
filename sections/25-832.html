§ 25-832 Prompt notice of investigative reports.

a
ABRA shall provide a licensee with either an investigative report or a public police incident report that may result in a show cause hearing as set forth in § 25-447 within 90 days of the date upon which the incident occurred.
b
The requirement in subsection (a) of this section shall not apply where:
1
Criminal action is being considered against the licensee or its employees; or
2
Enforcement action is requested by the Chief of Police under § 25-827.
