§ 47-1334 Interest rate.

The rate of interest on all amounts due, owing, or paid for purposes of redemption under this chapter (whether relating to real property sold or bid off to the District) shall be 1.5% per month or portion thereof. No interest shall accrue for surplus, expenses, or the reasonable value of improvements.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 14-282 substituted "1.5% per month or portion thereof" for "18% per month".
