The DC Code: § 2-218.42 Required programs, procedures, and policies to achieve contracting and procurement goals.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

To achieve the goals set forth in this subchapter, the Department shall establish by rules issued pursuant to § 2-218.72, programs for certified business enterprises. The Department shall include among these programs:

1
A bid preference mechanism for certified business enterprises with principal offices located in an enterprise zone;
2
A set-aside program for small business enterprises; and
3
A set-aside program for certified business enterprises for the District of Columbia Supply Schedule.
