The DC Code: § 32-1613 Disclosure of information by the Council.

Index → 32 Labor. (Refs & Annos)

The Council shall not withhold information from the public regarding its operations, procedures, and decisions that would otherwise be subject to disclosure under subchapter II of Chapter 5 of Title 2.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 17-353 validated a previously made technical correction by substituting "Council" for "Board". Legislative History of Laws For Law 13-150, see notes following § 32-1601. For Law 17-353, see notes following § 32-101. DC CODE § 32-1613 Current through December 11, 2012
