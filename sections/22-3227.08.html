The DC Code: § 22-3227.08 Police reports

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)

The Metropolitan Police Department shall make a report of each complaint of identity theft and provide the complainant with a copy of the report.

Emergency Act Amendments For temporary (90 day) addition, see § 2(c) of Identity Thief Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2004 (D.C. Act 15-388, March 18, 2004, 51 DCR 3382).
