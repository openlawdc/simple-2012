The DC Code: § 42-507 Estate in possession.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

An estate in possession exists when the owner has an immediate right to the possession of the land.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-207. 1973 Ed., § 45-807. DC CODE § 42-507 Current through December 11, 2012
