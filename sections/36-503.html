§ 36-503 Licensing and taxation.

Except as provided by 19 USC §§ 81a-81u, or by other Federal or District law, all activities and entities operating within a foreign trade zone that is established within the District of Columbia shall be subject to all applicable District licenses, permits, and taxation.
