§ 29-402.03 Incorporation.

a
Unless a delayed effective date is specified, the corporate existence begins when the articles of incorporation are filed.
b
The filing of the articles of incorporation by the Mayor is conclusive proof that the incorporators satisfied all conditions precedent to incorporation except in a proceeding by the state to cancel or revoke the incorporation or involuntarily dissolve the nonprofit corporation.
