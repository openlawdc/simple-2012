§ 29-1110 Effect of judgment or order.

A judgment or order against an unincorporated nonprofit association shall not by itself a judgment or order against a member or manager.

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 10 of the Uniform Unincorporated Nonprofit Association Act (2008 Act) .
