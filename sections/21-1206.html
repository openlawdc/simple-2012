§ 21-1206 Fees.

The Mayor is authorized to charge a registration fee to applicants certified as a trained medication employee.

Historical and Statutory

Temporary Amendments of Section Section 2 (e) of D.C. Law 13-32 added this section pertaining to fees. Section 4 (b) of D.C. Law 13-32 provides that the act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
