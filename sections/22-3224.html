§ 22-3224 Fraudulent registration.

a
A person commits the offense of fraudulent registration if, with intent to defraud the proprietor or manager of a hotel, motel, or other establishment which provides lodging to transient guests, that person falsely registers under a name or address other than his or her actual name or address.
b
Any person convicted of fraudulent registration shall be fined not more than $300 or imprisoned for not more than 90 days, or both.
