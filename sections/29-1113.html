§ 29-1113 Member meeting, voting and notice requirements.

a
Unless an unincorporated nonprofit association's governing principles provide otherwise:
1
Approval of a matter by members shall require an affirmative majority of the votes cast at a meeting of members; and
2
Each member shall be entitled to one vote on each matter that is submitted for approval by members.
b
Notice and quorum requirements for member meetings and the conduct of meetings of members shall be determined by the governing principles.
