§ 29-808.03 Proper plaintiff.

a
Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) of this section, a derivative action under § 29-808.02 shall be maintained only by a person that is a member at the time the action is commenced and remains a member while the action continues.
b
If the sole plaintiff in a derivative action dies while the action is pending, the Superior Court may permit another member of the limited liability company to be substituted as plaintiff.
