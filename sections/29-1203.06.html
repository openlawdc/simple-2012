§ 29-1203.06 Duration.

a
A statutory trust shall have perpetual duration.
b
A statutory trust, or any series thereof, shall not be terminated or revoked except in accordance with this chapter or the terms of the governing instrument.
c
The death, incapacity, dissolution, termination, or bankruptcy of a beneficial owner or trustee shall not result in the termination or dissolution of a statutory trust or any series thereof.
d
A statutory trust or any series thereof shall not terminate because the same person is the sole trustee and sole beneficial owner.
