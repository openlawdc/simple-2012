The DC Code: § 44-110.01 General conditions.

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
An ALR shall meet applicable zoning, building, housing, sewer, water, fire prevention codes, rules, and regulations of the District of Columbia.
b
An ALR shall maintain all structures, installed equipment, grounds, and individual living units in good repair and operable.
c
An ALR may be classified as a residential occupancy and may be located in a single or multi-family dwelling.
