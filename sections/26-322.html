The DC Code: § 26-322 Authority of Superintendent to issue rules and regulations.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

The Superintendent may promulgate such rules and regulations as deemed necessary and appropriate to implement the provisions of this chapter.
