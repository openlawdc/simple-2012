§ 3-506 Fees; expiration date of licenses.

a
The Mayor shall establish, increase, or decrease, fees as may be necessary to cover the costs of administering this subchapter. The Mayor shall not revise the fees prior to the Mayor giving a 30-day notice of the intended fee change.
b
The Mayor may, after a 30-day notice, establish and change, as may be necessary, the expiration date of licenses provided for in this subchapter. Upon the change of an expiration date, the renewal fee for licenses shall be prorated on the basis of the time covered.
