§ 42-1003 Special power.

A power is special:

1
Where the persons or class of persons to whom the disposition of the lands under the power is to be made is designated;
2
Where the power authorizes the alienation, by means of a conveyance, will, or charge, of a particular estate or interest less than a fee.
