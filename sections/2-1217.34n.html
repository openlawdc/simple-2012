The DC Code: § 2-1217.34n Fiscal impact of issuance.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of determining the fiscal impact of this subpart, the Bonds shall be deemed to be issued under the authority of subchapter IX-A of Chapter 12 of this title, as a project within the 7th Street/Georgia Avenue, N.W., Retail Priority Area established pursuant to the Great Streets Neighborhood Retail Priority Areas Approval Resolution of 2007, effective July 10, 2007 (Res. 17-257; 54 DCR 7194).
