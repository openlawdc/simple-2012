§ 29-1201.06 Rule of construction.

a
This chapter shall be liberally construed to give maximum effect to the principle of freedom of contract and to the enforceability of governing instruments.
b
The presumption that a civil statute in derogation of the common law is construed strictly shall not apply to this chapter.
