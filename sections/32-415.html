The DC Code: § 32-415 Rules and regulations; delegation of authority.

Index → 32 Labor. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall issue, and may amend from time to time, rules and regulations to implement the provisions of this chapter.
b
By issuance of a Mayor's Order, the Mayor may delegate the powers and duties assigned to the Mayor by this chapter.
