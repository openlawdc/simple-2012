§ 32-331.01 Grant and contract authority.

The Rehabilitation Services Program may:

1
Receive and administer grants, as necessary, to provide services to assist individuals with disabilities in achieving or maintaining gainful employment; and
2
Enter into contracts or other agreements, as necessary, to provide services or support to assist individuals with disabilities in achieving or maintaining gainful employment.
