The DC Code: § 29-1005.04 No liability as member for association's obligations.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

Unless the articles of organization otherwise provide, a debt, obligation, or other liability of a limited cooperative association shall be solely that of the association and shall not be the debt, obligation, or liability of a member solely by reason of being a member.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 504 of the Uniform Limited Cooperative Association Act .
