The DC Code: § 27-102 Liability and damages.

Index → 27 Civil Recovery by Merchants for Criminal Conduct. (Refs & Annos)
a
Anyone who commits an offense of fraud, shoplifting, or theft from a merchant shall be civilly liable to the merchant for treble the amount of actual damages; and
1
The retail value of any goods or merchandise stolen if the goods or merchandise are not recovered;
2
The loss of value of the goods or merchandise stolen if the goods or merchandise are recovered; or
3
A minimum of $50 in damages, whichever is greater.
b
The parent or guardian shall be liable for any acts or offenses committed by a juvenile under this chapter.
