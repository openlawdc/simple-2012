The DC Code: § 7-1203.05a Disclosures to correctional institutions or law enforcement officials.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
A mental health professional or mental health facility may disclose to a correctional institution or a law enforcement official having lawful custody of an individual mental health information about the individual to facilitate the delivery of mental health services and mental health supports to the individual.
b
Any disclosure of mental health information under this section shall be limited to the minimum necessary to facilitate the delivery of mental health services and mental health supports.
