The DC Code: § 16-2106 Separate composition or compromise.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

Any one of several joint debtors when their debt is overdue, may make a separate composition or compromise with their creditors, with the same effect as is provided in the case of parties by Chapter 3 of Title 41.
