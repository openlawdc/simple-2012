The DC Code: § 7-126 Executive committee.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

There is established an executive committee, which shall:

1
Be part of the Mayor's Council;
2
Advise the Mayor's Council;
3
Be led by the chairperson designated pursuant to § 7-121(b)(2);
4
Be comprised of not more than 10 members of the Mayor's Council; provided, that at least 3 members are members appointed by the Council, pursuant to § 7-121(b)(3)(A)(i), and 3 members are members appointed by the Mayor, pursuant to § 7-121(b)(3)(A)(ii); and
5
Meet no fewer than 4 times per year, and at least one time each quarter of the year.
