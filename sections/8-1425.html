§ 8-1425 Penalties.

a
Any person who violates § 8-1423 or rules issued under § 8-1426 shall be subject to a civil penalty not to exceed:
1
$10,000 for a first offense; or
2
$25,000 for any subsequent offense.
b
The fines assessed and collected under subsection (a) of this section shall be deposited into the General Fund of the District of Columbia.
