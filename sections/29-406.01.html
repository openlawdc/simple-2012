The DC Code: § 29-406.01 Requirement for and functions of board of directors.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
A nonprofit corporation shall have a board of directors.
b
Except as otherwise provided in § 29-406.12, all corporate powers shall be exercised by or under the authority of the board of directors of the nonprofit corporation, and the activities and affairs of the corporation shall be managed by or under the direction, and subject to the oversight, of its board of directors.
