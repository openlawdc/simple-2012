§ 22-3225.06 Indemnity.

An insurer shall not be liable for the following:

1
Damages or restitution provided by this subchapter, either jointly, severably, or as a third party, for insurance fraud offense committed by an insured; or
2
The defense of an insured or other person who is charged with insurance fraud.
