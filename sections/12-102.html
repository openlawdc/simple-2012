The DC Code: § 12-102 Substitution of parties.

Index → 12 Right to Remedy. (Refs & Annos)

The substitution of parties in civil actions in the United States District Court for the District of Columbia and the Superior Court of the District of Columbia is governed by the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure.
