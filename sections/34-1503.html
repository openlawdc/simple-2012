The DC Code: § 34-1503 Competitive billing

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)
a
Competitive billing shall begin on the initial implementation date and shall be implemented according to a schedule to be determined by the Commission. The Commission shall have the discretion to implement competitive billing for all customers on a single date.
b
This section shall not preclude the Commission from including competitive billing as a part of any pilot program established by the Commission.
