The DC Code: § 4-205.37 Standard for income and resource determination.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall, in establishing the need of an individual for assistance, take into consideration all income and resources of such individual in excess of any amounts which may, under the provisions of this chapter, be legally disregarded.
b
Repealed.
