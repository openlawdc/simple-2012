§ 34-2202.20 No inurement to private persons; exemption from District taxation.

a
All rights, property and assets of the Authority shall transfer automatically to the District government upon dissolution of the Authority.
b
None of the property, assets or earnings of the Authority shall inure to any private person or entity.
c
The Authority, its income, property, and transactions shall be exempt from District taxes.
