The DC Code: § 42-804 Survival of mortgagee's or trustee's title.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

Whenever a mortgage or deed of trust to secure a debt is executed to 2 or more mortgagees or trustees in fee simple, upon the death of any 1 or more of them the legal title and the trust attached to it shall be held to survive to the survivor or survivors and the heirs of the last survivor, subject to the provisions aforesaid.
