The DC Code: § 50-2708 Applicability.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)
a
This chapter shall apply to all vehicles towed by a private tow company under § 50-2702(a) after June 22, 2006.
b
This chapter shall also apply to all vehicles towed prior to June 22, 2006; provided, that notice is sent to the owners in accordance with § 50- 2702(a) or published in a newspaper of general circulation in accordance with § 50-2702(b).
