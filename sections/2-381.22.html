§ 2-381.22 Electronic procurement.

a
The CPO may issue a solicitation by any electronic medium, including the Internet, electronic mail, or disk medium.
b
The CPO may accept responses to solicitations by any electronic medium, including the Internet, electronic mail, or disk medium.
