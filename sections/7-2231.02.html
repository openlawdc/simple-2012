The DC Code: § 7-2231.02 Definitions.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this part, the term:

1
"Agency" means the Homeland Security and Emergency Management Agency.
2
"Director" means the Director of the Homeland Security and Emergency Management Agency.
3
"Program" means the Homeland Security Program created by § 7-2231.03.
