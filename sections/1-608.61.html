The DC Code: § 1-608.61 Rulemaking.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

The Attorney General may adopt rules to implement the provisions of this subchapter in accordance with subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 1-609.61. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 16-33 substituted "Attorney General" for "Corporation Counsel".
