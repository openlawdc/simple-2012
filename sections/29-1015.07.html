§ 29-1015.07 Consolidation.

a
Constituent limited cooperative associations may agree to call a merger a consolidation under this subchapter.
b
All provisions governing mergers or using the term merger in this chapter shall apply equally to mergers that the constituent associations choose to call consolidations under subsection (a) of his section.
