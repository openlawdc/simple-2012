§ 21-2001 Rule of construction; purposes.

a
This chapter shall be liberally construed and applied to promote its underlying purposes and policies.
b
The underlying purposes and policies of this chapter are to:
1
Simplify and clarify the law concerning the affairs of missing individuals, protected individuals, and incapacitated individuals;
2
Promote a speedy and efficient system for managing and protecting the estates of protected individuals so that assets may be preserved for application to the needs of protected individuals and their dependents; and
3
Provide a system of general and limited guardianships for incapacitated individuals and coordinate guardianships and protective proceedings concerned with management and protection of estates of incapacitated individuals.
