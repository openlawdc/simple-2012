§ 29-1203.02 Statutory trust as entity.

A statutory trust shall be an entity separate from its trustees and beneficial owners.

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 302 of the Uniform Statutory Trust Entity Act .
