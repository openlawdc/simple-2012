The DC Code: § 42-305 Title conveyable by anyone claiming such.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

Any person claiming title to land may convey his interest in the same, notwithstanding there may be an adverse possession thereof.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-305. 1973 Ed., § 45-105. Miscellaneous Notes Severance of joint tenancies: This section and § 42-301 do not supersede the common law rule authorizing the unilateral severance of joint tenancies.
