The DC Code: § 34-710 Fines, penalties and forfeitures to be paid into Treasury.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)

All moneys received from fines, forfeitures, and penalties shall be paid into the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the General Fund of the District of Columbia.
