Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 47-3102. 1973 Ed., § 47-3202. Legislative History of Laws For legislative history of D.C. Law 2-114, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 47-3101. Law 12-261, the "Second Omnibus Regulatory Reform Amendment Act of 1998," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 12-615, which was referred to the Committee of the Whole. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 1, 1998, and December 15, 1998, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on December 31, 1998, it was assigned Act No. 12-615 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 12-261 became effective on April 20, 1999.
