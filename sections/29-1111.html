The DC Code: § 29-1111 Member has no agency power.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A member of an unincorporated nonprofit association shall not be an agent of the association solely by reason of being a member.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 15 of the Uniform Unincorporated Nonprofit Association Act (2008 Act) .
