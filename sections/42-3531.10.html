The DC Code: § 42-3531.10 Rulemaking authority.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

On or before December 1, 2007, the Office of the Chief Tenant Advocate shall promulgate rules, subject to Council approval, to implement the provisions of this chapter.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For Law 16-181, see notes following § 42-3531.01. Effective Dates Section 5 of D.C. Law 16-181 provides: "Section 2 through 4 shall apply as of October 1, 2007." DC CODE § 42-3531.10 Current through December 11, 2012
