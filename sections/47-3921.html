The DC Code: § 47-3921 Applicability.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

The provisions of § 22 shall apply as of July 1, 1986. All other sections of this act shall apply as of March 1, 1989.
