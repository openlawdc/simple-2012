The DC Code: § 42-519 Estates from year to year.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

An estate expressed to be from year to year shall be good for 1 year only.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-219. 1973 Ed., § 45-819. DC CODE § 42-519 Current through December 11, 2012
