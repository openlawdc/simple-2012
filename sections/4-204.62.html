§ 4-204.62 Medicaid reimbursement system for nursing facilities.

a
The Department of Health shall develop and implement a case mix reimbursement methodology for nursing facilities. The case mix reimbursement methodology shall be effective no earlier than April 1, 2005.
b
The Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement this section.
