The DC Code: § 50-1104 Vehicles exempt from fee.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)

All motor vehicles and trailers owned and officially used by the government of the United States, the government of the District of Columbia or the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority shall be subject to inspection.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 40-204. 1973 Ed., § 40-204. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 13-289 rewrote the section which had read: "All motor vehicles and trailers owned and officially used by the government of the United States or by the government of the District of Columbia or by the representatives of foreign governments, shall be subject to annual inspection, such inspections to be furnished without charge."
