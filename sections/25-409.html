The DC Code: § 25-409 Application for importation permit.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)

The application for issuance or renewal of a importation permit shall include:

1
The quantity, character, and brand or trade name of the alcoholic beverage to be transported; and
2
The name and address of the retailer.
