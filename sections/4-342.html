§ 4-342 Definitions.

For the purpose of this subchapter, the term:

1
"Attorneys' fees" means the legal costs and expenses which are directly related to the adoption of a foster child or foster children.
2
"Foster care" means 24 hour substitute care for children placed away from their parents or guardians for whom the Child and Family Services Agency has placement care and responsibility.
3
"Foster child" and "foster children" mean a child, or children, who comes under the jurisdiction of the Superior Court of the District of Columbia pursuant to § 16-2320 or whose parents' rights have been relinquished pursuant to § 4-1406.
4
"Foster parent" means an individual with whom a foster child is legally placed.
5
"Homestudy" means the "investigation, report and recommendation" required by § 16-307.
6
"Related sibling group" means a group of siblings with at least one parent in common, residing together in the home of a foster parent.
