The DC Code: § 7-2071.12 Funding for the Ombudsman Program.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
Funding sources for the Ombudsman Program shall include:
1
District local appropriations; and
2
Medicaid federal matching funds.
b
Nothing in this chapter shall prohibit a corporation, organization, or consortia of organizations selected to operate the Health Care Ombudsman Program from raising private money through foundation resources to supplement government funds for the program.
