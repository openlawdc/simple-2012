§ 4-261.03 LIHEAP Heat and Eat initiative.

a
The Mayor shall establish a LIHEAP Heat and Eat initiative for the purpose of providing the maximum standard utility allowance to all participants.
b
All food stamp program recipients shall be automatically enrolled in the LIHEAP Heat and Eat initiative.
c
All LIHEAP Heat and Eat participants shall receive a minimum annual benefit of $1.
d
Participation in the LIHEAP Heat and Eat initiative shall not preclude any recipient from receiving standard LIHEAP benefits for which he or she is eligible.
