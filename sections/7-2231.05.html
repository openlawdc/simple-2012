The DC Code: § 7-2231.05 District of Columbia government employee security training program.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Director, in consultation with other District of Columbia agencies, law enforcement, security, and terrorism experts, and representatives of public employees, shall develop and issue guidelines for a public employee security training program to meet requirements established in the District of Columbia Emergency Response Plan.
b
At the request of the Director, District government agencies shall submit employee training programs to the Director for annual review.
