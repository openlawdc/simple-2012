§ 29-102.04 Withdrawal of filed record before effectiveness.

a
The parties to a filed record may withdraw the record before it takes effect.
b
To withdraw a filed record, the parties to the record shall deliver to the Mayor for filing a statement of withdrawal.
c
A statement of withdrawal shall:
1
Except as otherwise agreed by the parties, be signed on behalf of each party that signed the filed record being withdrawn;
2
Identify the filed record to be withdrawn, the date of its filing, and the parties to it; and
3
If not filed by all parties, state that the filed record has been withdrawn in accordance with the agreement of the parties.
d
On the delivery for filing to the Mayor of a statement of withdrawal, the action or transaction evidenced by the original filed record shall not take effect.
