The DC Code: § 19-315 No distinction between whole- and half-blood.

Index → 19 Descent, Distribution, and Trusts. (Refs & Annos)

There is no distinction between the kindred of the whole- and the half-blood.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 19-315. 1973 Ed., § 19-315. DC CODE § 19-315 Current through December 11, 2012
