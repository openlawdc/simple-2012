§ 3-342.01 Definitions.

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term "Baseball Stadium" shall have the same meaning as that provided for the term "Ballpark" in § 47- 2002.05(a)(1)(A).

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 17-169, the "Ballpark Public Safety Amendment Act of 2008", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No.17-584 which was referred to the Committee on Public Safety and Judiciary.  The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 4, 2008, and April 1, 2008, respectively.   Signed by the Mayor on April 14, 2008, it was assigned Act No. 17-343 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.  D.C. Law 17-169 became effective on June 5, 2008. DC CODE § 3-342.01 Current through December 11, 2012
