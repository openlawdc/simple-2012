The DC Code: § 31-5607.08 Provisions applicable to administrative proceedings

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)
a
All actions of the Commissioner, including administrative proceedings, adoption of rules, and issuance of orders shall be governed by subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2; provided, that:
1
The issuance of a stop order under § 31-5603.03(e) shall be governed by § 31-5603.03(e); and
2
The issuance of a summary order under § 31-5606.02(a) shall be governed by § 31-5606.02(a).
