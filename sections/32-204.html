The DC Code: § 32-204 Employment of minors under 16 years of age in certain occupations prohibited; exception.

Index → 32 Labor. (Refs & Annos)
a
No minor under 16 years of age shall be employed, permitted, or suffered to work at any of the following occupations:
1
In the operation of any machinery operated by power other than hand or foot power; or
2
In oiling, wiping, or cleaning machinery or assisting therein.
b
This section does not apply to any duly approved vocational education program or training under the auspices of the Board of Education or the Trustees of the University.
