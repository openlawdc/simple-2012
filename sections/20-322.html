The DC Code: § 20-322 When mandatory.

Index → 20 Probate and Administration of Decedents' Estates. (Refs & Annos)

A proceeding for standard probate shall be instituted:

a
if, at any time before abbreviated probate,
1
an interested person or creditor makes a request; or
2
it appears to the Court that the petition for abbreviated probate is materially incomplete or incorrect in any respect; or
b
in accordance with the provisions of section 20-331.
