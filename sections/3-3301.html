§ 3-3301 Commission; established. [Expired]

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 2-3601. Legislative History of Laws Law 7-156, the "Commission on Domestic Partnership Benefits for District of Columbia Government Employees Establishment Act of 1988," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 7-465, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 14, 1988 and June 28, 1988, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 15, 1988, it was assigned Act No. 7-211 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. DC CODE § 3-3301 Current through December 11, 2012
