The DC Code: § 42-203 Persons who may bring actions.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)
a
An action affecting a conservation easement may be brought by 1 of the following:
1
An owner of an interest in the real property burdened by the easement;
2
A holder of the easement;
3
A person having a third-party right of enforcement; or
4
A person authorized by other law.
b
This chapter does not affect the power of a court to modify or terminate a conservation easement in accordance with the principles of law and equity.
