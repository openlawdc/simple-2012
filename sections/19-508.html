The DC Code: § 19-508 Uniformity of application and construction.

Index → 19 Descent, Distribution, and Trusts. (Refs & Annos)

This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among the states enacting it.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For D.C. Law 13-292, see notes following § 19-501. Uniform Law This section is based upon § 8 of the Uniform Simultaneous Death Act (1993 Act). See 8B Uniform Laws Annotated, Master Edition, or ULA Database on WESTLAW. DC CODE § 19-508 Current through December 11, 2012
