The DC Code: § 44-108.04 Short-term residential care.

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

For individuals who will stay in the ALR no longer than 30 days, only the following information is required for admission to the ALR:

1
An analysis of the individual's current physical condition, medical status, and functional assessment as set out in this subchapter; and
2
A resident agreement in accordance with this chapter.
