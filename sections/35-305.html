The DC Code: § 35-305 Certain prior laws not affected.

Index → 35 Railroads and Other Carriers. (Refs & Annos)

Nothing in §§ 35-301 to 35-304, inclusive, shall be held to limit the duty of common carriers by railroads or impair the rights of their employees under the Safety Appliance Act of March 2, 1893, as amended April 1, 1896, and March 2, 1903.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 44-405. 1973 Ed., § 44-405. References in Text The Act of March 2, 1893, as amended, referred to in this section, was codified as 45 U.S.C. § 1 et seq., and was repealed in 1994 by P.L. 103-272, § 7(b). For present law, see 49 U.S.C. § 20301 et seq. DC CODE § 35-305 Current through December 11, 2012
