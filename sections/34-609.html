The DC Code: § 34-609 Method of review exclusive.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)

The method of review of the orders and decisions of the Commission provided by §§ 34-604 to 34-608, herein, shall be exclusive.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 43-909. 1973 Ed., § 43-710. Editor's Notes Former § 43-910 [1981 Ed.], 49 Stat. 885, ch. 742, § 4, provided that if any provision this section or the application to any person or circumstances is held invalid, the invalidity of the remainder of said sections and of the application of such provision to other persons and circumstances shall not be affected thereby.
