The DC Code: § 31-2709 Violations.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

Any company, broker, or agent guilty of violating any of the provisions of this chapter or any order, rule, or regulation issued pursuant to this chapter shall be subject to the provisions of §§ 31-2502.03 and 31-2502.36, respectively.
