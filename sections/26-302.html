The DC Code: § 26-302 Requirement of license.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

Except as provided in § 26-303 no person, including a person doing so on May 12, 1998, shall engage in the business of cashing checks for consideration without first obtaining a license from the Superintendent pursuant to this chapter. No separate license under this chapter shall be required for any agent of a licensee.
