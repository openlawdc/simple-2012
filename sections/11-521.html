The DC Code: § 11-521 Appointment of Auditor.

Index → 11 Organization and Jurisdiction of the Courts. (Refs & Annos)

For so long as the business of the court may require, the United States District Court for the District of Columbia may appoint an Auditor for the court.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 11-521. 1973 Ed., § 11-521. DC CODE § 11-521 Current through December 11, 2012
