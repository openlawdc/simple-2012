The DC Code: § 29-913 Voting--Number permitted by each member.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
Each member of an association shall have only one vote, except as may be altered in the articles or bylaws for:
1
Voting by member organizations; or
2
Allocation of votes in a cooperative housing association proportionate to the share of ownership in the association or on the basis of one vote for each unit.
b
A voting agreement or other device to evade the requirements of this section shall not be enforceable.
