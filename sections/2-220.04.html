The DC Code: § 2-220.04 Contents of contract; notice to subcontractors.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
a
All contracts and government assistance subject to this subchapter shall include the requirements under §§ 2-220.03, 2-220.06, 2-220.07, and 2-220.08.
b
Each recipient of a contract or government assistance shall notify each subcontractor subject to this subchapter of the requirements as provided under subsection (a) of this section. The notification shall be in writing.
