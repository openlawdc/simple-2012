The DC Code: § 5-1309 Protection of emergency 2-way radio communications--Forfeiture of equipment.

Index → 5 Police, Firefighters, Medical Examiner, and Forensic Sciences. (Refs & Annos)
a
Any 2-way radio and related equipment used to commit a violation of §§ 5-1306 to 5-1309 shall be subject to forfeiture.
b
Property subject to forfeiture under §§ 5-1306 to 5-1309 may be seized by law-enforcement officials, as designated by the Mayor, upon process issued by the Superior Court of the District of Columbia having jurisdiction over the property, or without process if authorized by other law.
