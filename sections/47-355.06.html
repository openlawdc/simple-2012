§ 47-355.06 Penalties.

An agency head, deputy agency head, agency fiscal officer, agency budget director, agency controller, manager, or other employee may be subject to adverse personnel action, including removal, for violating any provision in § 47-355.02.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 15-105, in par. (1), validated previously made technical corrections. D.C. Law 16-293, rewrote this section, which formerly read: "An agency head, deputy agency head, agency chief financial officer, agency budget director, agency controller, manager, or other employee may be subject to adverse personnel action, including removal, for: "(1) Violating § 47-355.02; "(2) Allowing an expenditure or obligation to exceed apportioned amounts; "(3) Not submitting a required plan or projection in a timely manner; "(4) Knowingly reporting incorrectly on spending to date or on projected total annual spending; or "(5) Failure to adhere to a spending plan." Legislative History of Laws For Law 14-285, see notes following § 47-355.01. For Law 15-105, see notes following § 47-340.22. For Law 16-293, see notes following § 47-355.02.
