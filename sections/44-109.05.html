§ 44-109.05 Medication administration.

a
Licensed nurses, physicians, physician assistants, and TMEs may administer medications to residents or assist residents with taking their medications.
b
(1) Each resident shall be identified prior to drug administration.
2
Drugs prescribed for one resident shall not be administered to another resident.
3
The TME shall report drug errors to the healthcare practitioner or licensed nurse and shall document the incident in the resident's record.
