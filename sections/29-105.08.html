§ 29-105.08 Withdrawal deemed on conversion to domestic filing entity or domestic limited liability partnership.

A qualified foreign entity registered to do business in the District which converts to any type of domestic filing entity or to a domestic registered limited liability partnership shall be deemed to have withdrawn its registration on the effective date of the conversion.
