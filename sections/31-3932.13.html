The DC Code: § 31-3932.13 Discount on reserves; report on reserves.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)
a
An SPFC may discount its reserves at discount rates as approved by the Commissioner.
b
An SPFC shall file annually an actuarial opinion on reserves provided by an approved independent actuary.
