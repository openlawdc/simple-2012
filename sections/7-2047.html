The DC Code: § 7-2047 Prosecutions.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
Prosecutions of violations of this subchapter shall be brought by the Corporation Counsel in the name of the District of Columbia.
b
In prosecutions initiated pursuant to this subchapter, a child development facility claiming an exemption from a licensing requirement of this subchapter shall have the burden of proving entitlement to the exemption.
