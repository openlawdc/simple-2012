The DC Code: § 16-1021 Definitions.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term:

1
"Child" means a person under the age of 16 years of age.
2
"District" means the District of Columbia.
3
"Lawful custodian" means a person who is authorized to have custody by an order of the Superior Court of the District of Columbia or a court of competent jurisdiction of any state, or a person designated by the lawful custodian temporarily to care for the child.
4
"Relative" means a parent, other ancestor, brother, sister, uncle, or aunt, or one who has been lawful custodian at some prior time.
