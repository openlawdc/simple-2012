The DC Code: § 31-207 Required annual reports of Commissioner--Publication and distribution.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

After May 18, 1910, the annual reports of the Commissioner of Insurance and Securities shall be printed and bound in 1 volume, and shall be ready for distribution not later than the 1st day of the next regular session of Congress thereafter.
