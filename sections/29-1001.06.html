§ 29-1001.06 Governing law.

The law of the District shall govern the:

1
Internal affairs of a limited cooperative association; and
2
Liability of a member as member and a director as director for the debts, obligations, or other liabilities of a limited cooperative association.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 107 of the Uniform Limited Cooperative Association Act .
