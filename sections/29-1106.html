§ 29-1106 Ownership and transfer of property.

a
An unincorporated nonprofit association may acquire, hold, encumber, or transfer in its name an interest in real or personal property.
b
An unincorporated nonprofit association may be a beneficiary of a trust or contract, a legatee, or a devisee.
