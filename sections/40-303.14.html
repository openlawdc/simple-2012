§ 40-303.14 Extent of ground bound by lien.

If there be any contest as to the dimensions of the land claimed to be subjected to the lien aforesaid, the court shall determine the same upon the evidence and describe the same in the decree of sale.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 38-116. 1973 Ed., § 38-116. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 16-31 substituted "land" for "ground". Legislative History of Laws For Law 16-31, see notes following § 40-301.03. DC CODE § 40-303.14 Current through December 11, 2012
