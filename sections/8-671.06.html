The DC Code: § 8-671.06 Notice.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)
a
A copy of an environmental covenant shall be provided by the persons designated by the agency and in the manner required by the environmental agency to:
1
Each person that signed the covenant;
2
Each person holding a recorded interest in the real property subject to the covenant;
3
Each person in possession of the real property subject to the covenant;
4
Each municipality or other unit of local government in which real property subject to the covenant is located; and
5
Any other person the environmental agency requires.
b
The validity of a covenant is not affected by failure to provide a copy of the covenant as required under this section.
