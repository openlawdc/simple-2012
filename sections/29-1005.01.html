§ 29-1005.01 Members.

To begin business, a limited cooperative association shall have at least 2 patron members unless the sole member is a cooperative.

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 501 of the Uniform Limited Cooperative Association Act .
