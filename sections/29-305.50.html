The DC Code: § 29-305.50 Definitions.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this part, the term:

1
"Derivative proceeding" means a civil action in the right of a domestic corporation or, to the extent provided in § 29-305.57, in the right of a foreign corporation.
2
"Shareholder" includes a beneficial owner whose shares are held in a voting trust or held by a nominee on the beneficial owner's behalf.
