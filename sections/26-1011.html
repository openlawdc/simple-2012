The DC Code: § 26-1011 Special reporting requirements.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

Within 15 days of the occurrence of any of the events listed below, a licensee shall file a written report with the Superintendent describing the event and its expected impact on the licensee's activities in the District:

1
The filing for bankruptcy or reorganization by the licensee;
2
The institution of revocation or suspension proceedings against the licensee by any state or governmental authority with regard to the licensees' money transmission activities;
3
Any felony indictment of the licensee or any of its key officers or directors related to money transmission activities; or
4
Any felony conviction of the licensee or any of its key officers or directors related to money transmission activities.
