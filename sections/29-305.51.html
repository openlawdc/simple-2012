The DC Code: § 29-305.51 Standing.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A shareholder shall not commence or maintain a derivative proceeding unless the shareholder:

1
Was a shareholder of the corporation at the time of the act or omission complained of or became a shareholder through transfer by operation of law from one that was a shareholder at that time; and
2
Fairly and adequately represents the interests of the corporation in enforcing the right of the corporation.
