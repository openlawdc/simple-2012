§ 44-208 Quality assurance.

a
The Mayor shall operate a laboratory reference system and shall prescribe standards for the examination of specimens.
b
The Mayor shall adopt rules pursuant to § 44-213 that:
1
Prohibit payment to laboratory personnel based upon the number of tests performed; and
2
Limit the number of hours that laboratory personnel may work.
c
The Mayor shall set standards for proficiency testing programs to determine a satisfactory result, a satisfactory level of overall performance, and a substandard level of overall performance.
