The DC Code: § 11-701 Continuation of court; court of record; seal.

Index → 11 Organization and Jurisdiction of the Courts. (Refs & Annos)
a
The District of Columbia Court of Appeals (hereafter in this subchapter referred to as the "court") shall continue as a court of record in the District of Columbia.
b
The court shall have a seal.
