The DC Code: § 3-1208.02 Licensed graduate social worker.

Index → 3 District of Columbia Boards and Commissions. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Board of Social Work shall license as a graduate social worker a person who, in addition to meeting the requirements of subchapter V of this chapter, has a master's degree or a doctorate from a social work program accredited by the Council on Social Work Education, and has satisfactorily completed the examination process at the graduate level.
b
A licensed graduate social worker ("L.G.S.W.") may perform any function described as the practice of social work in this chapter, other than psychotherapy, under the supervision of a social worker licensed under § 3- 1208.03 or § 3-1208.04, and may perform psychotherapy under the supervision of a social worker licensed under § 3-1208.04.
