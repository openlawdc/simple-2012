The DC Code: § 29-1205.06 Good-faith reliance.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A trustee, officer, employee, manager, or committee of a statutory trust, or other person designated pursuant to § 29-1201.03(e)(8), shall not be liable to the trust or to a beneficial owner for breach of any duty, including a fiduciary duty, to the extent the breach results from good-faith reliance on:

1
A term of the governing instrument;
2
A record of the statutory trust; or
3
An opinion, report, or statement of another person that the trustee reasonably believes is within the other person's professional or expert competence and is made or delivered to the trustee, officer, employee, manager, or committee of a statutory trust or other person designated pursuant to § 29-1201.03(e)(8).
