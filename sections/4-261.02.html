§ 4-261.02 Categorical eligibility for food stamps.

a
The Mayor shall establish a TANF funded program or service for the purpose of establishing categorical eligibility.
b
Categorical eligibility shall be granted to all applicants with a gross income at or below 200% of the federal poverty level.
