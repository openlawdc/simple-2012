The DC Code: § 48-831.32 Eligibility of the uninsured.

Index → 48 Foods and Drugs. (Refs & Annos)

To be eligible, an individual shall:

1
Be a resident of the District;
2
Have a household income that is not more than 350% of the federal poverty level; and
3
Not be enrolled in any public or private medical insurance program.
