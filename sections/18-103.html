The DC Code: § 18-103 Execution of written will; attestation.

Index → 18 Wills. (Refs & Annos)

A will or testament, other than a will executed in the manner provided by section 18-107, is void unless it is:

1
in writing and signed by the testator, or by another person in his presence and by his express direction; and
2
attested and subscribed in the presence of the testator, by at least two credible witnesses.
