§ 31-5705 Service of process on nonresident associations.   [Repealed]

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 35-1205. Legislative History of Laws Law 10-128, the "Omnibus Budget Support Act of 1994," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 10-575, which was referred to the Committee of the Whole. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 22, 1994, and April 12, 1994, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on April 14, 1994, it was assigned Act No. 10-225 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 10-128 became effective on June 14, 1994. For legislative history of D.C. Law 10-233, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 31-5703. For legislative history of D.C. Law 11-268, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 31-5702.
