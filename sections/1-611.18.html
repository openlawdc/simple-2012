The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on May 7, 1991, and June 4, 1991, respectively. Law 9-33 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 9-215, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 4, 1991, and June 18, 1991, respectively.
