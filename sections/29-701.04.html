§ 29-701.04 Nature, purpose, and duration of entity.

a
A limited partnership is an entity distinct from its partners. A limited partnership is the same entity regardless of whether its certificate states that the limited partnership is a limited liability limited partnership.
b
A limited partnership may be organized under this chapter for any lawful purpose.
c
A limited partnership shall have a perpetual duration.
