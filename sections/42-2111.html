The DC Code: § 42-2111 Notice.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

Pursuant to rules issued in accordance with § 42-2104, the Mayor shall give:

1
Reasonable advance notice to the record owners and affected parties of properties brought to tax sale in accordance with § 47-1205 (b) and (c); and
2
Reasonable advance notice of properties scheduled to be sold and the date of sale by advertising the list of properties in a newspaper of general circulation published in the District of Columbia at least once every 2 weeks.
