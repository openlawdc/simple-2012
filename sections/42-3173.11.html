The DC Code: § 42-3173.11 Use of funds; deposit of funds.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)
a
Amounts collected by the District of Columbia under this subchapter shall be deposited into the fund established by § 42-3131.01(b)(1).
b
Amounts in the fund established by § 42-3131.01(b)(1), may be used to pay the costs incurred by the District to demolish or enclose a structure under this subchapter.
