§ 10-1905 Transfer to public benefit conveyance applicant.

Notwithstanding Chapter 8 of this title, the Mayor is authorized to transfer the subject real property to the applicable public benefit conveyance applicant in accordance with the terms of the applicable Memorandum of Agreement.
