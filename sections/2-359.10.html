The DC Code: § 2-359.10 Employees subject to employee conduct standards of Merit Personnel Act.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
a
Except for those District government employees employed by agencies not subject to Chapter 6 of Title 1, District government employees who participate in the procurement process shall be subject to the provisions of subchapter XVIII of Chapter 6 of Title 1.
b
Participation in the procurement process shall include involvement, either directly or indirectly, in:
1
The decision, approval, disapproval, recommendation, or preparation of any part of a purchase request;
2
Influencing the content of any specification or purchase standard;
3
Rendering of advice;
4
An investigation or audit; or
5
Any other advisory capacity pertaining to any contract, subcontract, solicitation, or proposal.
