The DC Code: § 9-1010 Separability.

Index → 9 Transportation Systems. (Refs & Annos)

Except as provided in § 9-1006(h), if any provision of this chapter or the application thereof to any person or circumstance, is held invalid, the remainder of this chapter and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances shall not be affected thereby.
