The DC Code: § 16-4416 Representation by lawyer.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

A party to an arbitration proceeding may be represented by a lawyer.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For Law 17-111, see notes following § 16-4401. Uniform Law This section is based upon § 16 of the Uniform Arbitration Act (2000). See Vol. 7, Pt. I, Uniform Laws Annotated, Master Edition, or ULA Database on Westlaw. DC CODE § 16-4416 Current through December 11, 2012
