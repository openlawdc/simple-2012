The DC Code: § 32-1611 One-stop partners.

Index → 32 Labor. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Council shall ensure that the District's one-stop delivery system under the Federal Act is the foundation of local service delivery to employers and participants.
b
One-stop partners shall include the Income Maintenance Administration, Office of Early Childhood Development, and the Medicaid program.
