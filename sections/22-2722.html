The DC Code: § 22-2722 Keeping bawdy or disorderly houses.

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)

Whoever is convicted of keeping a bawdy or disorderly house in the District shall be fined not more than $5,000 or imprisoned not more than 5 years, or both.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 22-2722. 1973 Ed., § 22-2722. Effect of Amendments D.C.
