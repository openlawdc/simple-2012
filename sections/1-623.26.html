§ 1-623.26 Subpoenas; oaths; examination of witnesses.

The Mayor, on any matter within his or her jurisdiction under this subchapter, shall have the authority to:

1
Issue subpoenas for and compel the attendance of witnesses within a radius of 100 miles of the District of Columbia;
2
Administer oaths;
3
Examine witnesses; and
4
Require the production of books, papers, documents, and other evidence.
