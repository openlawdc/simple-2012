The DC Code: § 4-205.43 Participation in labor dispute; pregnancy.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
Repealed.
b
A pregnant woman may be eligible for TANF benefits for herself if the pregnancy has been medically certified, the pregnancy is in the third trimester, and other eligibility requirements are met. The Mayor may provide to the pregnant woman written information and referral as to the availability of prenatal care services and nutrition supplements for pregnant women.
