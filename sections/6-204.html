The DC Code: § 6-204 Tax exemption.

Index → 6 Housing and Building Restrictions and Regulations. (Refs & Annos)
a
All assets and income of the Authority and all Housing Properties (whether or not owned or operated by the Authority) shall be exempt from District taxation, subject to the conditions contained in subsection (b) of this section and the approval required by subsection (c) of this section. Absent an agreement with the Authority approved by the Council, for-profit activities shall not be exempt from District taxation.
b
The Authority is empowered to negotiate tax exemption agreements concerning for-profit activities. These tax exemption agreements shall be limited to full or partial relief from the following District taxes: property, income, and sales. The maximum duration of any tax exemption agreement under this section shall be 5 years.
c
Before a tax exemption agreement involving for-profit activities can become effective, legislation for this purpose shall be submitted to the Council for approval by act.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 15-337, in subsecs. (a) and (b), substituted "for-profit activities" for "for-profit activities involving Housing Properties".
