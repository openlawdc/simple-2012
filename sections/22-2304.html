§ 22-2304 Penalties.

a
Any person convicted of violating any provision of § 22-2302 shall be fined not more than $300 or be imprisoned not more than 90 days or both.
b
In lieu of or in addition to the penalty provided in subsection (a) of this section, a person convicted of violating any provision of § 22-2302 may be required to perform community service as provided in § 16-712.
