The DC Code: § 31-2231.25 Regulations.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

The Commissioner may, in accordance with § 2-505, promulgate reasonable rules, regulations, or orders as are necessary or appropriate to carry out and effectuate the provisions of this chapter.
