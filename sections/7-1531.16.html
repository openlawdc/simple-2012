The DC Code: § 7-1531.16 Other prohibited acts.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

A person that, in order to obtain a financial gain, intentionally falsifies, forges, conceals, defaces, or obliterates a document of gift, an amendment or revocation of a document of gift, or a refusal commits a felony and upon conviction is subject to a fine not exceeding $50,000, imprisonment not exceeding 5 years, or both.
