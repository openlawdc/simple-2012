§ 42-3671.06 Prohibited practices.

A lease-purchase agreement shall not contain:

1
A confession of judgment;
2
A negotiable instrument;
3
A security interest or any other claim of a property interest in any goods except those goods delivered by the lessor under the lease-purchase agreement;
4
A wage assignment;
5
A waiver by the consumer of claims or defenses; or
6
A provision authorizing the lessor, or the lessor's agent, to enter upon the consumer's premises or to commit any breach of the peace in the repossession of goods.
