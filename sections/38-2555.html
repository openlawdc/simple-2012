The DC Code: § 38-2555 Memorandum of Understanding.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
The voting members of the Task Force shall adopt and sign a Memorandum of Understanding regarding the implementation of the Reform Plan, which shall be submitted to the Council.
b
This chapter shall expire upon the submission of the Memorandum of Understanding to the Council pursuant to subsection (a) of this section.
