§ 21-2203 Presumption of capacity.

An individual shall be presumed capable of making health-care decisions unless certified otherwise under § 21-2204. Mental incapacity to make a health-care decision shall not be inferred from the fact that an individual:

1
Has been voluntarily or involuntarily hospitalized for mental illness pursuant to § 21-501 et seq.;
2
Has a diagnosis of an intellectual disability or has been determined by a court to be incompetent to refuse commitment under § 7-1301.01 et seq.; or
3
Has a conservator or guardian appointed pursuant to § 21-1501 et seq. or § 21-2001 et seq.
