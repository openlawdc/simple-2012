§ 9-103.06 Inapplicability of §§ 9-103.01 to 9-103.05 to Interstate System.

None of the provisions of §§ 9-103.01 to 9-103.05 shall apply to any segment of the Interstate System within the District of Columbia.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 7-112. 1973 Ed., § 7-112a. References in Text "Section 9-103.05", referred to in the catchline and text, was repealed by § 711 of D.C. Law 4-201, effective March 10, 1983. DC CODE § 9-103.06 Current through December 11, 2012
