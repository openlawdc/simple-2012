§ 31-3932.03 Application of subchapter I of this chapter.

a
Except as otherwise provided, §§ 31-3931.03, 31-3931.04, 31- 3931.05, 31-3931.09, 31-3931.10, 31-3931.11, 31-3931.12, 31- 3931.13, 31-3931.14, 31-3931.15, 31-3931.16, 31-3931.17, 31- 3931.18, 31-3931.19, 31-3931.20, 31-3931.21, and 31-3931.22 shall apply under this subchapter to SPFCs.
b
The Commissioner, by rule or order, may exempt an SPFC, on a case-by-case basis, from the provisions of this title that the Commissioner determines to be inappropriate given the nature of the risks to be insured.
