§ 29-705.05 No distribution on account of dissociation.

A person shall not have a right to receive a distribution on account of dissociation.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 505 of the Uniform Limited Partnership Act (2001 Act).
