The DC Code: § 1-612.10 Termination of medical emergency.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

The medical emergency affecting a leave recipient shall terminate when:

1
The leave recipient's employment terminates; or
2
The leave recipient is no longer affected by the medical emergency.
