§ 8-1059 Hearings.

Any person adversely affected by an action taken pursuant to the provisions of this subchapter, or the rules and regulations issued under its authority, shall be entitled to a hearing before the Mayor upon filing with the Mayor, within 15 days of the date of the action, a written request for a hearing. The hearing shall be held in accordance with § 2-509.
