§ 3-1206.22 Required disclosures.

Unless also licensed by the Board of Medicine to practice medicine in the District, practitioners of naturopathic medicine shall:

1
Provide to the client or patient, before providing services, a written notice in a language the client or patient understands that the practitioner is not licensed to practice medicine; and
2
Obtain written acknowledgment from the client or patient that the client or patient has been provided the notice required in paragraph (1) of this section.
