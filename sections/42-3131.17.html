§ 42-3131.17 Transmission of list of blighted vacant buildings by Mayor.

a
Semiannually, the Mayor shall transmit to the Office of Tax and Revenue a list of buildings designated by the Mayor as blighted vacant buildings for which a notice of final determination has been issued under this and administrative appeals have been exhausted or expired.
b
The list shall be in the form and medium prescribed by the Office of Tax and Revenue.
