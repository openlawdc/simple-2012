The DC Code: § 1-307.82 Establishment of the District of Columbia Medical Liability Captive Insurance Agency.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)
a
There is established, as a subordinate agency under the Mayor, the District of Columbia Medical Liability Captive Insurance Agency.
b
The purpose of the Agency is to provide medical malpractice liability insurance policies for health centers, including coverage for the staff, contractors, and volunteer service providers for the services provided at the health centers. The liability of the Agency for medical malpractice liability insurance policies shall be limited to the funds in the Medical Liability Captive Trust Fund.
