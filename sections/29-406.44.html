§ 29-406.44 Contract rights of officers.

a
The appointment of an officer shall not itself create contract rights.
b
An officer's removal shall not affect the officer's contract rights, if any, with the nonprofit corporation. An officer's resignation shall not affect the corporation's contract rights, if any, with the officer.
