The DC Code: § 47-2210 Returns and payment of tax.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

The provisions of §§ 47-2015, 47-2016, 47-2017, and 47-2018 are hereby incorporated in and made applicable to this chapter. Every vendor, and every vendor or retailer not engaging in business in the District who is expressly authorized to pay the tax, shall file returns and pay the tax in accordance with the provisions of such sections applicable to the filing of returns and the payment of the tax and as shall be prescribed by regulation.
