The DC Code: § 47-212 Items included in annual estimates--Expenses of Water Department.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

It shall be the duty of the Mayor to include in the annual estimates of the District of Columbia estimates of the expenses of the Water Department.
