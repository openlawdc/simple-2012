The DC Code: § 50-2537 Mount Pleasant Visitor Pass Pilot Program.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall implement a one-year visitor parking pilot program for residential permit parking areas within ANC1D boundaries.
b
For the purposes of this pilot program, DDOT may:
1
Charge a fee for each permit issued pursuant to this program; and
2
Limit the hours for which a visitor parking permit is valid.
c
Within 90 days of November 25, 2008, the Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of this section.   The proposed rules shall be submitted to the Council for a 30-day period of review.  If the Council does not approve or disapprove the proposed rules, by resolution, within the 30-day period, the rules shall be deemed approved.
