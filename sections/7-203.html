The DC Code: § 7-203 Appointment and duties of Registrar.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

The Director of the Department of Health shall appoint the Registrar who shall:

1
Be in charge of administering the vital records system and be the custodian of its records; and
2
Develop and distribute forms or other means for transmitting data to carry out the reporting and registration purposes of this chapter.
