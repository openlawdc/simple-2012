The DC Code: § 29-1208.01 Events causing dissolution.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A statutory trust shall be dissolved only by:

1
An administrative dissolution under §§ 29-106.01 and 29-106.02; or
2
The filing of articles of dissolution under § 29-1208.02:
A
On the occurrence of an event or circumstance that the governing instrument states causes dissolution; or
B
With the approval of all the beneficial owners.
