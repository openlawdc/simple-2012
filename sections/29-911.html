§ 29-911 Meetings; regular and special--Notice.

The secretary shall give notice of the time and place of meetings by sending a notice thereof to each member at the member's last known address not less than the number of days in advance of the meeting specified in the bylaws. In case of a special meeting, the notice shall specify the purpose for which the meeting is called.
