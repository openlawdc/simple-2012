§ 29-101.04 Delivery of record.

a
Except as otherwise provided in this title, permissible means of delivery of a record include delivery by hand, mail by the United States Postal Service, commercial delivery service, and electronic transmission.
b
Delivery to the Mayor shall be effective only when the record is received by the Mayor.
