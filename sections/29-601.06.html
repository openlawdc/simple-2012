§ 29-601.06 Governing law.

a
Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) of this section or § 29- 611.01(a), the law of the jurisdiction in which a partnership has its principal office shall govern relations among the partners and between the partners and the partnership.
b
The law of District of Columbia shall govern relations among the partners and between the partners and the partnership and the liability of partners for an obligation of a limited liability partnership.
