§ 29-1208.02 Articles of dissolution.

a
If dissolution of a statutory trust is authorized under § 29-1208.01, the trust shall deliver to the Mayor for filing articles of dissolution setting forth the:
1
Name of the trust; and
2
Date of the dissolution.
b
Except as otherwise provided in § 29-102.03, a statutory trust is dissolved when articles of dissolution that comply with subsection (a) of this section, are filed by the Mayor.
