The DC Code: § 46-306.03 Effect of registration for enforcement.

Index → 46 Domestic Relations. (Refs & Annos)
a
A support order or income-withholding order issued in another state is registered when the order is filed in the registering tribunal of the District.
b
A registered order issued in another state is enforceable in the same manner and is subject to the same procedures as an order issued by a tribunal of the District.
c
Except as otherwise provided in this subchapter, a tribunal of the District shall recognize and enforce, but may not modify, a registered order if the issuing tribunal had jurisdiction.
