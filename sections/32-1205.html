§ 32-1205 Enforcement by civil action.

a
Subject to the provisions in subsection (b) of this section, an employee or the Mayor may bring a civil action against any employer to enforce the provisions of this chapter in any court of competent jurisdiction.
b
No civil action may be commenced more than 1 year after the occurrence or discovery of the alleged violation of this chapter.
c
If a court determines that an employer violated any provision of this chapter, the damages provision prescribed in § 32-1204(b)(6) and (c) shall apply.
