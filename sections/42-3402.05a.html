The DC Code: § 42-3402.05a Application fees.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor may impose and collect fees for the processing of an application for conversion and other services provided by the Mayor or the Department of Housing and Community Development to implement this chapter. The Mayor shall establish the fees by rulemaking pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2.
b
Each application for approval of a conversion shall be accompanied by payment to cover the fees, if any, prescribed pursuant to this section.
c
Fees collected by the Mayor pursuant to this section shall be deposited in the Department of Housing and Community Development Unified Fund, established pursuant to § 42-2857.01.
