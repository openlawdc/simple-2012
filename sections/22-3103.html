The DC Code: § 22-3103 Penalties.

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)

Violation of this chapter shall be a felony and shall be punished by:

1
A fine of not more than $5,000 or imprisonment for not more than 10 years, or both for the first offense; or
2
A fine of not more than $15,000 or imprisonment for not more than 20 years, or both for the 2nd and each subsequent offense.
