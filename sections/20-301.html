The DC Code: § 20-301 Scope.

Index → 20 Probate and Administration of Decedents' Estates. (Refs & Annos)

This chapter applies to the opening of an estate which begins with the filing of a petition for probate by an interested person as provided in section 20- 304 and results in the probate of a will or the determination of a decedent's intestacy and the appointment of a personal representative. Probate may occur either:

a
As an abbreviated probate proceeding as provided in subchapter II of this chapter;
b
As a standard probate proceeding as provided in subchapter III of this chapter;
c
As a small estates proceeding as provided in subchapter VI of this chapter.
