§ 38-1903 Teachers in the Americanization schools; custodial staff.

a
Officers and teachers in the Americanization, evening, and summer schools may also be officers and teachers in the regular day schools.
b
Members of the custodial staff in the evening, summer, and Americanization schools may also be members of the custodial staff in the day schools.
