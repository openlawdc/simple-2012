The DC Code: § 5-1501.08 Transfer of personnel, records, functions, and authority.

Index → 5 Police, Firefighters, Medical Examiner, and Forensic Sciences. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall provide for the orderly transfer to the Department all of the authority, responsibilities, duties, assets, and functions of MPD pertaining to forensic science services, including:
1
Forensic alcohol;
2
Computer forensics;
3
Analysis of controlled substances;
4
DNA/biological material analysis;
5
Fingerprint comparison;
6
Firearms and tool mark examination;
7
Forensic photography;
8
Analysis of questioned documents;
9
Trace evidence analysis;
10
Personnel and authority for vacant and filled positions;
11
Property;
12
Records; and
13
All unexpended balances of appropriations, allocations, and other funds available or to be made available to the MPD for the purposes of forensic science services.
b
The transfer set forth in subsection (a) of this section shall occur no later than October 1, 2012.
