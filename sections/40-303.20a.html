The DC Code: § 40-303.20a Authority to promulgate regulations.

Index → 40 Liens. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall promulgate rules to implement this chapter. The proposed rules shall be transmitted to the Council for a 45-day period of review, excluding Saturdays, Sundays, legal holidays, and days of Council recess. If the Council does not approve or disapprove the rules within the 45-day review period, the rules shall be deemed disapproved.
b
The Mayor shall issue rules to implement the provisions of D.C. Law 16-31, within 180 days of October, 20, 2005.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 16-31 rewrote section, which had read as follows: "The Mayor shall promulgate rules to implement §§ 40-301.01 through 40-303.20 in accordance with subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2."
