The DC Code: § 19-305 Distribution of surplus after payment to surviving spouse or surviving domestic partner.

Index → 19 Descent, Distribution, and Trusts. (Refs & Annos)

The surplus, above the share of the surviving spouse or surviving domestic partner, or the whole surplus, when there is no surviving spouse or surviving domestic partner, descends and is distributed as provided by this chapter and by section 19-701.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 19-305. 1973 Ed., § 19-305. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 16-79, in the section heading, substituted "surviving spouse or surviving domestic partner" for "surviving spouse"; and substituted "surviving spouse or surviving domestic partner" for "surviving spouse" throughout section.
