The DC Code: § 25-371 Moratorium on establishments which permit nude dancing.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)
a
Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section, no licensee under this title shall permit nude dancers.
b
A licensee who regularly provided entertainment by nude dancers before December 15, 1993, may continue to do so at its establishment.
