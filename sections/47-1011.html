The DC Code: § 47-1011 Property of United States, District of Columbia, and foreign legations exempt from assessments for improvements.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

No property except that of the United States or the District of Columbia and property owned by foreign governments for legation purposes shall be exempt from assessments for improvements.
