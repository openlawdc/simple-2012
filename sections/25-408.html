§ 25-408 Application for a tasting permit for a class A licensee.

The application for issuance or renewal of a tasting permit for off-premises retailer's license, class A, shall include:

1
A copy of the applicant's off-premises's retailers license, class A;
2
Drawings of the premises indicating the areas where sampling is to occur; and
3
The hours and days during which the tasting is to occur.
