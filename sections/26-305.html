The DC Code: § 26-305 Application and license fees.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

At the time of filing an application under this chapter, each applicant shall pay to the Superintendent a non-refundable license fee of $300, except that an applicant for a license to maintain one or more limited stations shall pay the non-refundable limited station license application fee as provided in § 26- 314.
