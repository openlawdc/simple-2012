§ 7-751.03 Administration of the Program.

a
The Department of Health shall administer the Program and shall:
1
Establish an application process;
2
Certify a list of acceptable service obligation sites on an annual basis and make the list publically available;
3
Conduct regular surveys to ensure participant compliance with the Program;
4
Disburse all awarded funds; and
5
Administer any other functions necessary to the Program.
b
The Department of Health reserves the right to conduct regular inspections to ensure that all service obligations sites meet the definition as set forth in § 7-751.01(9).
