The DC Code: § 4-1303.03e Behavioral health screening and assessment requirements.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
All children in the custody of the Agency shall, to the extent that it is not inconsistent with a court order, receive a behavioral health screening and, if necessary, a behavioral health assessment within 30 days of initial contact with the Agency or a placement disruption. Through rulemaking, the Mayor may reduce the number of days within which a behavioral health screening and behavioral health assessment are required.
b
The Agency shall connect all children who are assessed as being in need of behavioral health care to an appropriate behavioral health service.
c
The Agency shall provide the behavioral health resource guide for parents and legal guardians and the behavioral health resource guide for youth created pursuant to § 7-1131.18 to families of children in Agency custody.
