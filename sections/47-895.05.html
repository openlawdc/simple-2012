The DC Code: § 47-895.05 Termination of Special Assessment.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

The Special Assessment shall terminate on the earlier of:

1
September 30, 2044; or
2
At the end of the fiscal year when all the bonds are paid for and are no longer outstanding pursuant to their terms; provided, that any delinquent Special Assessments and related penalties and interest shall remain due until fully paid.
