The DC Code: § 50-507 Other rights or remedies; limitations on actions.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)
a
Nothing in this chapter shall in any way limit the rights or remedies which are otherwise available to a consumer under any other law.
b
Any action brought pursuant to this chapter shall be commenced within 4 years of the date of original delivery of the motor vehicle to the consumer.
