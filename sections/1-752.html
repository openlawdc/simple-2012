§ 1-752 Application of procedure.

a
The procedure established by this subchapter shall apply to any participant or beneficiary who has a claim for benefits denied under the District of Columbia Police Officers and Fire Fighters' Retirement Fund, established by § 1-712; the District of Columbia Teachers' Retirement Fund, established by § 1-713; and the District of Columbia Judges' Retirement Fund, established by § 1-714.
b
The procedure established by this subchapter shall apply to claims for benefits denied after March 1, 1982.
