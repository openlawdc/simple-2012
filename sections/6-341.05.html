The DC Code: § 6-341.05 Definitions.

Index → 6 Housing and Building Restrictions and Regulations. (Refs & Annos)

As used in this subchapter, the terms "Agency," "lessee," "purchaser," "real property," "redevelopment," and "redevelopment company" shall have the respective meanings provided for such terms by § 6-301.02.
