The DC Code: § 50-711 Labeling regulations.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)

The Mayor shall issue regulations establishing labeling requirements for clean-fuel vehicles operated by fleets in the covered areas.
