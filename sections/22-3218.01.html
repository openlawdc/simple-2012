§ 22-3218.01 Definitions.

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term:

1
"Company" means a person or enterprise engaged in the generation or distribution of natural gas or electricity.
2
"Person" means any individual, corporation, company, association, firm, partnership, joint stock company, or other entity.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 14-310, the "Criminal Code and Miscellaneous Technical Amendments Act of 2002", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 14-954, which was referred to the Committee on Whole.  The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 3, 2002, and December 17, 2002, respectively.   Signed by the Mayor on January 22, 2003, it was assigned Act No. 14-622 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.  D.C.  Law 14-310 became effective on June 12, 2003.
