The DC Code: § 7-2005 Repayment.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
For each loan issued under this subchapter, the Mayor shall arrange a repayment schedule.
b
Each loan granted from the fund shall be recorded as a lien against the real and personal property of the applicant.
