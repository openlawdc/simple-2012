§ 47-1303.03 Tax deed.

a
The Mayor shall issue a deed for the bid off property sold pursuant to § 47-1303.02 to the person whose offer the Mayor accepts.
b
The deed shall be prima facie evidence of a good and perfect title in fee simple to the bid off property.
