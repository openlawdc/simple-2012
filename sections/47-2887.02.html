The DC Code: § 47-2887.02 Service of process; subpoenas.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)
a
By acting as an athlete agent in the District of Columbia, a nonresident individual appoints the Mayor as the individual's agent for service of process in any civil action in the District of Columbia related to the individual's acting as an athlete agent in the District of Columbia.
b
The Mayor may issue subpoenas for any material that is relevant to the administration of this part.
