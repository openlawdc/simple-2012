The DC Code: § 42-3531.04 Definitions.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this chapter, the term:

1
"Chief" means Chief Tenant Advocate established by § 42-3531.06.
2
"Office" means the Office of the Tenant Advocate established by § 42- 3531.05.
3
"TAC" means Tenant Advisory Council.
4
"Tenant" and "tenant organization" shall have the same meaning as in § 42-3401.03(17) and (18), and shall include any other tenant organization.
