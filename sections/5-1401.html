The DC Code: § 5-1401 Definitions.

Index → 5 Police, Firefighters, Medical Examiner, and Forensic Sciences. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this chapter, the term:

1
"District" means the District of Columbia.
2
"Legal custody" includes imprisonment, jail, or detention.
3
"Ward" means any person in the official custody of the District government, on a temporary or permanent basis, because of neglect, abuse, mental illness or intellectual disability.
