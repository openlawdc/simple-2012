§ 29-707.01 Partner's transferable interest.

Except as otherwise provided in subchapter X of this chapter or Chapter 2 of this title, the only interest of a partner which is transferable shall be partner's transferable interest. The interest of a partner, whether or not transferable, shall be personal property.
