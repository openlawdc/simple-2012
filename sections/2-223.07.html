The DC Code: § 2-223.07 Applicability.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
a
This subchapter shall apply to actions taken after July 13, 1998.
b
This subchapter shall apply to employees of the WMATA when the Commonwealth of Virginia and the State of Maryland enact similar provisions for WMATA whistleblowers.
