The DC Code: § 8-151.04 Director; appointment, compensation.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)
a
The DDOE shall be headed by a Director who shall:
1
Be appointed by the Mayor with the advice and consent of the Council, pursuant to § 1-523.01(a);
2
Be a person qualified by training and experience to perform the duties of the office; and
3
Serve at the pleasure of the Mayor.
b
The Mayor shall fix the compensation of the Director pursuant to subchapter X-A of Chapter 6 of Title 1.
