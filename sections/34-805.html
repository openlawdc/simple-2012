The DC Code: § 34-805 People's Counsel--Appropriations.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)

There are authorized to be appropriated, to carry out the purposes of §§ 34- 804 and 34-912, such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 1980 and for each fiscal year thereafter.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 43-407. 1973 Ed., § 43-205a. Legislative History of Laws Law 3-34 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 3-70, which was referred to the Committee on Public Services and Consumer Affairs. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on July 17, 1979 and July 31, 1979, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on August 31, 1979, it was assigned Act No. 3-100 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. DC CODE § 34-805 Current through December 11, 2012
