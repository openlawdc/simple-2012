The DC Code: § 34-1262.01 Prohibition of discrimination.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)
a
A cable operator shall not refuse to hire or employ, discharge, or otherwise discriminate against any person for any reason provided in Unit A of Chapter 14 of Title 2.
b
The provisions of the Human Rights Act shall apply to a cable operator.
