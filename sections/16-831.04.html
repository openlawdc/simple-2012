§ 16-831.04 Third-party custody orders.

a
A custody order entered under this chapter may include any of the following:
1
Sole legal custody to the third party;
2
Sole physical custody to the third party;
3
Joint legal custody between the third party and a parent;
4
Joint physical custody between the third party and a parent; or
5
Any other custody arrangement the court determines is in the best interests of the child.
b
An order granting relief under this chapter shall be in writing and shall recite the findings upon which the order is based.
