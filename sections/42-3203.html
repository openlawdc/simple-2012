The DC Code: § 42-3203 Notice of termination--Tenancies at will.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

A tenancy at will may be terminated by 30 days notice in writing by either landlord or tenant.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-1403. 1973 Ed., § 45-903. DC CODE § 42-3203 Current through December 11, 2012
