§ 29-809.11 Subchapter not exclusive.

This subchapter shall not preclude a limited liability company from being merged under law other than this chapter.

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 1015 of the Uniform Limited Company Act (2006 Act).
