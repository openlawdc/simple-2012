The DC Code: § 10-141 Rock Creek Park--Area.

Index → 10 Parks, Public Buildings, Grounds, and Space. (Refs & Annos)

The total area of lands finally to be acquired for said parkway shall not exceed the area and parcels described and delineated on map numbered 2, contained in House Document Numbered 1114 of the 64th Congress, First Session.
