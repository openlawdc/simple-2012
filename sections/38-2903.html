The DC Code: § 38-2903 Foundation level.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

The foundation level or cost of providing public education services is $9,124 per student for fiscal year 2013 and subsequent fiscal years. The foundation level may be revised in subsequent years in accordance with provisions for inflation, revenue unavailability, and periodic review and revision of the Formula, pursuant to §§ 38-2909, 38-2910, and 38-2911.
