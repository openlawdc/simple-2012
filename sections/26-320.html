The DC Code: § 26-320 Formal investigations.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

Whenever it appears that an individual or entity licensed or required to be licensed under this chapter has violated or is violating any law, agreement, order, rule, or regulation or has engaged in an unsafe or unsound practice, the Superintendent may issue an order to conduct a formal investigation of such person. The Superintendent may cause any investigation, or portion of such investigation, to be conducted by a District or federal law enforcement agency by making the request for assistance from such agency.
