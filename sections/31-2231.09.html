The DC Code: § 31-2231.09 Stock operations and advisory board contracts.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

No person, as an inducement to purchase insurance, shall issue or deliver, or permit its agents, officers, or employees to issue or deliver:

1
Agency company stock or other capital stock, benefit certificates, or shares in a common law corporation;
2
Securities; or
3
A special or advisory board contract or other contracts of any kind promising returns and profits.
