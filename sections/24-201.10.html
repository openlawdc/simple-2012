The DC Code: § 24-201.10 Detention of United States prisoners in Washington Asylum and Jail.

Index → 24 Prisoners and Their Treatment. (Refs & Annos)

The Department of Corrections is hereby authorized and directed to receive and keep in the Washington Asylum and Jail all prisoners committed thereto for offenses against the United States.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 24-410. 1973 Ed., § 24-410. References in Text "Department of Corrections" was substituted for "Board of Public Welfare" pursuant to § 24-211.03. DC CODE § 24-201.10 Current through December 11, 2012
