The DC Code: § 29-106.01 Grounds.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

The Mayor may commence a proceeding under § 29-106.02 to dissolve a domestic filing entity administratively if the entity does not:

1
Pay any fee, tax, or penalty required to be paid to the Mayor not later than 5 months after it is due;
2
Deliver a biennial report to the Mayor not later than 6 months after it is due; or
3
Have a registered agent in the District for 60 days.
