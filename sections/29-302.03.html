§ 29-302.03 Incorporation.

a
Unless a delayed effective date is specified, the corporate existence shall begin when the articles of incorporation are filed.
b
The Mayor's filing of the articles of incorporation shall be conclusive proof that the incorporators satisfied all conditions precedent to incorporation, except in a proceeding by the District to cancel or revoke the incorporation or involuntarily dissolve the corporation.
