The DC Code: § 1-622.07 Group insurance; amounts.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)
a
The group life insurance amounts purchased by the District under § 1- 622.05 shall be no less than the insurance amounts provided under the Federal Employees Group Life Insurance ("F.E.G.L.I.") plan pursuant to 5 U.S.C. § 8702, in effect as of October 1, 1987.
b
Employees shall be offered the option of purchasing additional coverage for themselves, and for their spouses and dependent children, and the cost of the additional coverage shall be borne solely by the employees purchasing that coverage, except Executive Service employees.
