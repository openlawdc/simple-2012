The DC Code: § 21-150 Proof.

Index → 21 Fiduciary Relations and Persons with Mental Illness. (Refs & Annos)

Every fact material to determine the propriety of a sale or exchange shall be clearly proved, in a proceeding brought pursuant to section 21-148, by disinterested witnesses, whose testimony shall be taken in writing in the presence of the guardian ad litem or upon interrogatories agreed upon by him.
