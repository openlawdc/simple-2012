The DC Code: § 34-2401.14 Payment of water rents from Washington Aqueduct into General Fund.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)

All water rents derived from the Washington Aqueduct shall be paid into the General Fund of the District of Columbia as established by the Revenue Funds Availability Act of 1975.
