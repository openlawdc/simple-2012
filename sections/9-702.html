The DC Code: § 9-702 Powers and duties of Administrator.

Index → 9 Transportation Systems. (Refs & Annos)

The Administrator shall have control over, and responsibility for, the care, operation, maintenance, and protection of the airport, together with the power to make and amend such rules and regulations as he may deem necessary to the proper exercise thereof.
