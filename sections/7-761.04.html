The DC Code: § 7-761.04 Organization.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Department shall have sufficient staff, supervisory personnel, and resources to accomplish the purposes of this chapter.
b
The Director shall have the authority to organize the Department as the Director may determine is necessary and appropriate to carry out the Department's mission.
