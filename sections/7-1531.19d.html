§ 7-1531.19d Effect of amendment or revocation of anatomical gift upon Registry.

a
If a donor amends an anatomical gift, or revokes one or more, but not all, anatomical gifts, the Registry shall be promptly revised to reflect the gift or gifts as amended.
b
If a donor revokes all anatomical gifts, the name of the former donor shall be promptly removed from the Registry.
