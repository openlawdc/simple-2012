§ 38-752.02 Definitions.

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term:

1
"Post-secondary institution" means an entity that awards an academic degree or professional certification, which may include a:
A
University;
B
College;
C
Seminary;
D
Vocational school;
E
Trade school; or
F
The military.
2
"Public high school" means a public school or public charter school that provides instruction for students in the 9th through 12th grades.
