§ 44-213 Rules.

a
The Mayor shall, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, issue proposed rules, including a schedule of civil fines, to implement the provisions of this chapter.
b
The Mayor may issue emergency rules, which shall be effective no more than 90 days and which shall be consistent with subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2.
