§ 47-1362 Fixing amount necessary for redemption after action to foreclose filed.

a
If the real property is redeemed after an action to foreclose the right of redemption is filed and there is a dispute regarding redemption, the person redeeming may apply to the Superior Court for an order fixing the amount necessary for redemption in accordance with the provisions of this chapter.
b
Except as provided in subsection (c) of this section, the Mayor may accept a payment for redemption without an order of court.
c
If there is a dispute regarding redemption, the Mayor shall not accept a payment for redemption unless a certified copy of the order of court fixing the amount necessary for redemption is filed with the Mayor.
