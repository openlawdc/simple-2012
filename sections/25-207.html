The DC Code: § 25-207 ABRA Director and staff.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)
a
The Board, with the advice and consent of the Council, shall appoint a Director of ABRA for a renewable 4-year term. The Director shall be removed by the Board for just and reasonable cause.
b
The Director shall organize the personnel and property transferred by § 25-203 and, within the limits provided in this chapter and annual appropriations, shall employ staff as needed to carry out the function of ABRA.
