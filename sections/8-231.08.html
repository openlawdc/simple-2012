The DC Code: § 8-231.08 Property owner's concurrent obligations.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)

The provisions of this chapter do not reduce, replace, or eliminate:

1
The duties and obligations of a property owner to monitor, repair, or maintain the property as required under any applicable District law or regulation; or
2
The authority of the Mayor to enforce applicable housing codes or to issue orders in accordance with any applicable District law or regulation.
