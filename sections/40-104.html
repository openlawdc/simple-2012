The DC Code: § 40-104 Application of proceeds of sale.

Index → 40 Liens. (Refs & Annos)

The proceeds of such sale shall be applied:

1
To the expenses of such sales and the discharge of such lien;
2
To payment of other liens, if any, in the order of their priority; and
3
To the owner of the property.
