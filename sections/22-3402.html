The DC Code: § 22-3402 Use of "District of Columbia" or similar designation by private detective or collection agency--Penalty.

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)

Any person who violates § 22-3401 shall be punished by a fine of not more than $300 or by imprisonment for not more than 90 days, or by both such fine and imprisonment.
