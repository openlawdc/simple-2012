The DC Code: § 10-161 Theodore Roosevelt Island--Designation.

Index → 10 Parks, Public Buildings, Grounds, and Space. (Refs & Annos)

In all public documents, records, and maps of the United States in which such island is designated or referred to it shall be designated as "Theodore Roosevelt Island."
