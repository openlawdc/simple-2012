§ 8-2010 Sick or injured horses.

a
The owner, operator, or custodian of a horse engaged in the horse-drawn carriage trade that is in pain, sick, diseased, lame, or injured shall take action to obtain immediate veterinary treatment, care, and attention for the horse.
b
An injured, sick, diseased, or lame horse shall not be sold or otherwise disposed of except in a humane manner.
c
No person shall drive, use, or work an injured, sick, diseased, or lame horse in the horse-drawn carriage trade.
