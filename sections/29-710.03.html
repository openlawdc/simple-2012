§ 29-710.03 Action on plan of merger by constituent limited partnership.

a
Subject to § 29-710.06, a plan of merger shall be consented to by all the partners of a constituent limited partnership.
b
Subject to § 29-710.06 and any contractual rights, after a merger is approved, and at any time before a filing is made under § 29-710.04, a constituent limited partnership may amend the plan or abandon the planned merger:
1
As provided in the plan; and
2
Except as prohibited by the plan, with the same consent as was required to approve the plan.
