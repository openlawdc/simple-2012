§ 29-406.03 Number of directors.

a
A board of directors shall consist of 3 or more directors, with the number specified in or fixed in accordance with the articles of incorporation or bylaws.
b
The number of directors may be increased or decreased, but to no fewer than 3, by amendment to, or in the manner provided in, the articles of incorporation or bylaws.
