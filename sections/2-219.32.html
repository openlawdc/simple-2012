§ 2-219.32 Definitions.

For the purposes of this part, the term:

1
"Executive Director" means the Executive Director of the Office of First Source Compliance.
2
"Office" means the Office of First Source Compliance.
3
"First Source Employment Agreement" means the requirements of part A of this subchapter.
