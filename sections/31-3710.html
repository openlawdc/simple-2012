The DC Code: § 31-3710 Rules.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall issue proposed rules to implement the provisions of this subchapter within 180 days of October 1, 1992. The proposed rules shall be submitted to the Council for a 45-day period of review, excluding Saturdays, Sundays, legal holidays, and days of Council recess. If the Council does not approve or disapprove the proposed rules, in whole or in part, by resolution within this 45-day period, the proposed rules shall be deemed approved. Nothing in this section shall affect any requirements imposed upon the Mayor by subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2.
b
The Mayor may issue emergency rules without prior Council approval, which shall be effective for not more than 120 days.
