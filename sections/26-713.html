§ 26-713 Hearings.

Any administrative hearing held in accordance with this subchapter shall be held pursuant to Chapter 5 of Title 2, unless the Superintendent determines that a public proceeding would jeopardize or adversely affect the safety and soundness of the bank or the public interest. If the Superintendent makes the determination, the hearing may be held in private session.
