The DC Code: § 7-1651.02 Human papillomavirus public education campaign.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

By January 1, 2008, the Mayor shall initiate a public information campaign, including multiple HPV vaccine education forums in each ward, aimed at educating the public on:

1
The connection between HPV and cervical cancer;
2
The importance of protecting oneself against HPV infection;
3
The value of screening for cervical cancer through regular pap tests; and
4
The effectiveness and risks of the HPV vaccine.
