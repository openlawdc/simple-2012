The DC Code: § 47-103 Appointed officers to give security for intrusted moneys.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

All officers appointed by the President for the District, who, by virtue of the provisions of any law of Congress, are required to give security for moneys that may be intrusted to them for disbursement, shall give such security at such time and in such manner as the Secretary of the Treasury may prescribe.
