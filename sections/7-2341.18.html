The DC Code: § 7-2341.18 Judicial review.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

A person or entity aggrieved by a decision of the Office of Administrative Hearings may appeal the decision to the District of Columbia Court of Appeals in accordance with Chapter 5 of Title 2 and pursuant to Chapter 18A of Title 2.
