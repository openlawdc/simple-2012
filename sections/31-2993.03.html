The DC Code: § 31-2993.03 Right to file grievance.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

This chapter shall not limit, prohibit, or modify a qualified individual's right to:

1
File a grievance and use an independent review process, if available; or
2
Use the independent medical review system.
