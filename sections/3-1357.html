The DC Code: § 3-1357 Payments into Fund.

Index → 3 District of Columbia Boards and Commissions. (Refs & Annos)

The following shall be deposited into the Fund:

1
All grants and revenues received by the Commission, including those received pursuant to § 3-1354(10).
2
Fines paid for violations of § 31-2413(a). The maximum amount of fines deposited into the Fund from this paragraph shall be $275,000 in fiscal year 2009, $750,000 in fiscal year 2010, $1 million in fiscal year 2011, and increased annually, beginning in fiscal year 2012, by 5%.
3
All interest earned on the deposits of the Fund.
