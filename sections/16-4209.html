The DC Code: § 16-4209 Participation in mediation.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)
a
An attorney or other individual designated by a mediation party may accompany the party to and participate in a mediation.
b
A waiver of participation given before the mediation may be rescinded.
