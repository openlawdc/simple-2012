The DC Code: § 47-2715 Glenn Dale Hospital rates.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

The per diem rate to be charged patients for medical care and service at Glenn Dale Hospital shall be $75.50.
