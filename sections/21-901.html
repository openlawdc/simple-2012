§ 21-901 Definition.

As used in this chapter, "mental illness" has the same meaning as that given to the term by section 21-501.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 21-901. 1973 Ed., § 21-901. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 19-169 substituted "mental illness" for "mentally ill person". Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 19-169, see notes under § 21-501. Miscellaneous Notes Section 35 of D.C. Law 19-169 provides: "Sec. 35.
