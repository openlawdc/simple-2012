§ 2-381.21 Electronic transactions.

a
Notwithstanding any other provisions of this chapter, the CPO may acquire supplies and services through:
1
Electronic solicitation and bid response; or
2
Electronic auctions.
b
In selecting one of the methods authorized by this section, upon proper validation and authorization, a contracting officer may accept electronic signatures for all electronic commerce transactions.
