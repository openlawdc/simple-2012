§ 4-205.75 POWER--Redetermination of eligibility.

a
A POWER recipient's eligibility for POWER shall be redetermined at intervals determined by the Mayor.
b
A POWER recipient, who is determined ineligible for POWER solely because the recipient is no longer incapacitated, or because other factors considered with the recipient's incapacity no longer substantially precludes the recipient's ability to work or to participate in job search or job readiness activities, shall be certified as eligible for TANF in a fashion that ensures financial assistance is not disrupted, if the recipient meets all TANF eligibility criteria. The Mayor shall provide adequate and timely notice that the POWER recipient has been determined ineligible for POWER.
