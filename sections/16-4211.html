The DC Code: § 16-4211 Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

This chapter modifies, limits, or supersedes the federal Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, approved June 30, 2000 (114 Stat. 464; 15 U.S.C. § 7001 et seq.), but this chapter does not modify, limit, or supersede section 101(c) of that act or authorize electronic delivery of any of the notices described in section 103(b) of that act.
