§ 6-1035 Procedures of the Commission.

a
The Director of the Department, or the Director's designee, shall convene the first meeting of the Commission during December 2002; provided, that at least 5 appointments have been made to the Commission.
b
The Commission shall meet at least once a month, at a time and place to be determined by the Chairperson. The Chairperson may call additional meetings.
c
A majority of the members shall constitute a quorum.
d
An audio or written transcript shall be kept of all meetings at which a vote is taken.
