The DC Code: § 29-801.05 Powers.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A limited liability company shall have the capacity to sue and be sued in its own name and the power to do all things necessary or convenient to carry on its activities.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 105 of the Uniform Limited Company Act (2006 Act).
