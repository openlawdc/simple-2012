§ 8-1403.01 Stops and inspection.

To determine compliance with this chapter and its implementing regulations, a police officer may stop the driver of a motor vehicle and enter upon the premises of a motor carrier that is regulated pursuant to this chapter and inspect any of the following:

1
All equipment, parts, and accessories, including carrier maintenance, certification, and safety records;
2
All driver records, including driver's license, permits, hours of service records, certificate of physical examination, and training records;
3
All manifests, including bills of lading or other shipping documents; and
4
All cargo and cargo areas, including the removal of cargo seals when necessary to conduct a safety inspection.

Historical and Statutory
