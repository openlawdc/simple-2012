The DC Code: § 31-756 Applicability; membership interest; powers.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)
a
A membership interest in a mutual insurance holding company shall not constitute an equity security as defined in § 31-603.
b
A mutual insurance holding company created under this subchapter shall have the same powers to borrow or assume liability as a reciprocal insurance company organized under District law.
