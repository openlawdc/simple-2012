§ 1-325.51 Funds for HIV/AIDS Crisis Area Capacity Building.

a
There is established a revolving HIV/AIDS Crisis Area Capacity Building Fund, to be administered by the Mayor, for the purposes of providing loans and grants of up to $500,000 to develop, support, expand, repair, or improve service delivery to persons with HIV/AIDS within those Wards that did not receive grants from the HIV/AIDS Administration during fiscal year 2005.
b
There is authorized to be appropriated out of the revenue of the District $500,000 to carry out the purposes of this part.
