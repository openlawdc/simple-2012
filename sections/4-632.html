The DC Code: § 4-632 Establishment of Healthy DC Program; administration.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
There is established the Healthy DC Program ("Program"), which shall provide affordable health benefits to eligible individuals.
b
The Program shall be administered by the Department of Health Care Finance, established by Chapter 7D of Title 7.
c
The Program shall be funded through the Healthy DC and Health Care Expansion Fund, established by § 31-3514.02.
d
(1) The Program shall be subject to the availability of funding.
2
Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to create or constitute an entitlement to health insurance or health or medical benefits.
