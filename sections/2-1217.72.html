§ 2-1217.72 Limitations on issuance of Bonds.

a
Bonds shall not be issued pursuant to this subchapter to the extent the issuance will cause the aggregate principal amount of Bonds issued pursuant to this subchapter and the TIF Act to exceed $500 million; provided, that the aggregate amount of TIF bonds for projects in the Central Business District, as defined in Title 11 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations, shall not exceed $300 million.
b
Bonds shall not be issued pursuant to this subchapter after September 30, 2015.
