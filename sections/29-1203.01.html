§ 29-1203.01 Governing law.

The law of the District shall govern the:

1
Internal affairs of a statutory trust;
2
Liability of a beneficial owner as beneficial owner and a trustee as trustee for a debt, obligation, or other liability of a statutory trust or a series thereof; and
3
Enforceability of a debt, obligation, or other liability of the statutory trust or a series thereof against the property of the trust or any series thereof.
