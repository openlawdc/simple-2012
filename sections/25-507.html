The DC Code: § 25-507 Minimum annual fee for solicitor's licenses.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)

The minimum annual fee for a solicitor's license shall be $250.

Historical and Statutory

D.C. Law 13-298 amended and enacted into law Title 25 of the District of Columbia Official Code. Legislative History of Laws For D.C. Law 13-298, see notes following § 25-101. DC CODE § 25-507 Current through December 11, 2012
