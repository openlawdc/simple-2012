§ 47-2855.04 Rules; fees.

a
The Mayor shall adopt rules as necessary to administer this subchapter. The rules may include the specifying of forms and the setting of fees for trade name registrations, amendments, searches, renewals, and copies of registration documents.
b
Fees set pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall not exceed the actual cost of administering this title; provided, that
1
For expedited same-day service, there shall be a fee of $100 in addition to other fees required by statute or rule;
2
For expedited 3-day service, there shall be a fee of $50 in addition to other fees required by statute or rule.
