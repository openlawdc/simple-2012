The DC Code: § 3-1328 Persons ineligible for suppliers' license.

Index → 3 District of Columbia Boards and Commissions. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Board, in its discretion, may determine the following persons not to be eligible to receive a suppliers' license: A person convicted of a felony who either has not received a pardon or has not been released from parole or probation for at least 5 years; a person who is or has been a professional gambler or gambling promoter; a public officer or employee; or a business in which a person disqualified under provisions of this section is employed or active or in which a person is married to, in a domestic partnership with, or related in the 1st degree of kinship to, such person who has an interest of more than 10 percent in the business.
b
For the purposes of this section, the term "domestic partnership" shall have the same meaning as provided in § 32-701(4).
