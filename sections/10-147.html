The DC Code: § 10-147 Potomac Park--Establishment.

Index → 10 Parks, Public Buildings, Grounds, and Space. (Refs & Annos)

The entire reclaimed area formerly known as the Potomac Flats, together with the tidal reservoirs, are made and declared a public park, under the name of the Potomac Park, and to be forever held and used as a park for the recreation and pleasure of the people.
