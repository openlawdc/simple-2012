§ 50-1331.05 Restrictions on Use and Transfer of Salvage Vehicles.

a
No Salvage Vehicle may be registered under subchapter I of Chapter 15 of this title.
b
Ownership of a Salvage Vehicle shall be transferred only through the use of a Salvage Title.
