§ 29-1205.02 Trustee powers.

A trustee may exercise:

1
Powers conferred by the governing instrument;
2
Except as limited by the governing instrument, any other powers necessary or convenient to carry out the business and affairs of the statutory trust; and
3
Other powers conferred by this chapter.
