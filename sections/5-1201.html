The DC Code: § 5-1201 Definitions.

Index → 5 Police, Firefighters, Medical Examiner, and Forensic Sciences. (Refs & Annos)

For purposes of this chapter:

1
"State" means the several states of the United States, Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, American Samoa, and Guam.
1-a
"State agent" means any person compensated directly or indirectly by a state or who in any way assists in the administration of the enforcement of laws of a state relating to alcoholic beverages, tobacco, or tobacco products.
2
"State official" means any agent, employee, or representative officially responsible for the administration and enforcement of laws of a state relating to alcoholic beverages, tobacco, and tobacco products.
