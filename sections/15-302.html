§ 15-302 Period during which writ of execution may issue; returnable period.

a
A writ of execution on a judgment in a civil action may be issued within three years after:
1
the expiration of any stay of execution agreed to by the parties; or
2
it first might have been issued under applicable provisions of law or rules of court.
b
A writ of execution shall be returnable on or before the sixtieth day after its date.
