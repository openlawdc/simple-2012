§ 29-102.03 Effective time and date.

Except as otherwise provided in § 29-102.04 and subject to § 29- 102.05(c), an entity filing shall be effective:

1
On the date and at the time of its filing by the Mayor as provided in § 29-102.06;
2
On the date of filing and at the time specified in the entity filing as its effective time, if later than the time under paragraph (1) of this section;
3
If permitted by this title, at a specified delayed effective time and date, which shall not be more than 90 days after the date of filing; or
4
If a delayed effective date as permitted by this title is specified, but no time is specified, at 12:01 a.m. on the date specified.
