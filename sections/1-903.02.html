§ 1-903.02 Retirement fund for teachers.

a
The Teachers' Retirement Fund shall continue in existence and shall be the fund into which the following deposits shall be made, which shall constitute the assets of the fund:
1
Any employee contribution amount paid to the Retirement Board pursuant to subchapter II of Chapter 20 of Title 38, or pursuant to § 38-2061.02;
2
Any asset transferred to the fund under subsection (b) of this section;
3
Any amount appropriated by the District government for the fund in accordance with § 1-905.02; and
4
Any return on investment of assets of the fund.
b
After June 30, 1997, annuities and other retirement and disability benefits (including refunds and lump-sum payments) for the benefits of covered teachers shall become assets of the fund.
