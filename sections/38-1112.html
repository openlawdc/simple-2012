The DC Code: § 38-1112 State consent requirement satisfied.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

The enactment of §§ 38-1107 and 38-1109 shall, as respects the District of Columbia, be deemed to satisfy any requirement of state consent contained in any of the laws or provisions of law referred to in such sections.
