§ 29-1015.08 Subchapter not exclusive.

This subchapter shall not prohibit a limited cooperative association from being merged under law other than this chapter.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 1612 of the Uniform Limited Cooperative Association Act .
