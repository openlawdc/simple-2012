The DC Code: § 19-1304.01 Methods of creating trust.

Index → 19 Descent, Distribution, and Trusts. (Refs & Annos)

A trust may be created by:

1
Transfer of property to another person as trustee during the settlor's lifetime or by will or other disposition taking effect upon the settlor's death;
2
Declaration by the owner of property that the owner holds identifiable property as trustee;
3
Exercise of a power of appointment in favor of a trustee; or
4
A court for the benefit of an individual in lieu of a transfer of property to a conservatorship or guardianship pursuant to Chapter 1 or subchapter VI of Chapter 20 of Title 21 of the District of Columbia Official Code.
