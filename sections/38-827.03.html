The DC Code: § 38-827.03 Rules of procedure.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Chairperson of the Commission, or his or her designated representative, who shall be a member of the Commission, shall convene all meetings of the Commission. Seven members of the Commission shall constitute a quorum. Voting by proxy shall not be permitted.
b
All meetings, reports, and recommendations shall be a matter of public record.
c
The Commission shall establish its meeting schedule; provided, that the Commission shall meet at least 4 times during each calendar year.
d
The Commission may establish subcommittees as needed. Subcommittees may include persons who are not members of the Commission; provided, that each subcommittee shall be chaired by a Commission member.
