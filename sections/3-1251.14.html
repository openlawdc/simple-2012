The DC Code: § 3-1251.14 Reports to the Board.

Index → 3 District of Columbia Boards and Commissions. (Refs & Annos)

The Board shall require reports from the Committee annually and at such other times as it believes may be necessary and appropriate. The reports shall include:

1
Information concerning the number of cases accepted, denied, and terminated, with compliance or noncompliance; and
2
A cost analysis of the Program.
