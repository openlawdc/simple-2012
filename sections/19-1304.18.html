§ 19-1304.18 Title of trust property.

Real or personal property that is transferred to a trust may be titled in the name of:

1
The trust by reference to the instrument creating the trust;
2
The current trustee as the trustee of such trust; or
3
"The trustee" as the trustee of such trust.
