The DC Code: § 16-4426 Jurisdiction.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)
a
A court of the District of Columbia having jurisdiction over the controversy and the parties may enforce an agreement to arbitrate.
b
An agreement to arbitrate providing for arbitration in the District of Columbia confers exclusive jurisdiction on the court to enter judgment on an award under this chapter.
