The DC Code: § 2-1208.04 Freedom of information act disclosure.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

All data collected and maintained as part of the reporting obligations imposed by this subchapter shall be fully subject to, and comply with, subchapter II of Chapter 5 of this title.
