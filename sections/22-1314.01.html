§ 22-1314.01 Definitions.

For the purpose of § 22-1314.02, the term:

1
"Health professional" means a person licensed to practice a health occupation in the District pursuant to § 3-1201.01.
2
"Medical facility" includes a hospital, clinic, physician's office, or other facility that provides health or surgical services.
3
"Person" shall not include:
A
The chief medical officer of the medical facility or his or her designee;
B
The chief executive officer of the medical facility or his or her designee;
C
An agent of the medical facility; or
D
A law enforcement officer in the performance of his or her official duty.
