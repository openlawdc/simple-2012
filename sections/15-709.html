The DC Code: § 15-709 Fees and costs in Superior Court.

Index → 15 Judgments and Executions; Fees and Costs. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Superior Court of the District of Columbia may prescribe fees and costs, including the fee to be paid for a jury trial.
b
Fees for services by the United States marshals for processes issued by the Superior Court shall be prescribed by rules of that court.
