The DC Code: § 2-139 Inspector of Elevators and Fire Escapes.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

One of the Assistant Inspectors of Buildings shall hereafter also perform the duties of Inspector of Elevators and Fire Escapes, without additional compensation.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 1-1027. 1973 Ed., § 1-729. Miscellaneous Notes New implementing regulations: Pursuant to this section, the following new regulations were adopted in 1984: The "Apartment House Elevator Act of 1984" (D.C.
