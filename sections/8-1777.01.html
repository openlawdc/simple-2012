§ 8-1777.01 Energy reduction.

a
The Mayor shall develop a plan that results in a 15% reduction in energy use by each District agency and instrumentality.
b
The plan shall:
1
Include specific recommendations on implementation, including the resources and the time period required to implement the plan;
2
Be submitted to the Council on or before December 31, 2009; and
3
Consider all sources of energy.
