The DC Code: § 2-1137 Indemnification.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
a
Any liability incurred by the Regional Authority, not covered by insurance under § 2-1136(c)(4)(C), shall be further indemnified by the Signatories, in proportion to the relative economic benefit currently expected to accrue to each Signatory from hosting the Olympic Games, as follows:
1
The State of Maryland shall be liable for 53%;
2
The Commonwealth of Virginia shall be liable for 19%; and
3
The District of Columbia shall be liable for 28%.
b
Each of the Signatories may provide for its share of any possible liability in any manner it may choose, as befits each Signatory's independent commitment.
