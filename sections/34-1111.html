The DC Code: § 34-1111 Commission to furnish blank forms.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)

The Commission shall cause to be prepared suitable blanks for carrying out the purposes of this subtitle, and shall when necessary furnish such blanks to each public utility.
