The DC Code: § 24-923 Location of BOOT CAMP.

Index → 24 Prisoners and Their Treatment. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Director shall use an existing building or set of buildings, which may be located in the Washington Metropolitan area, to establish a residential center for the BOOT CAMP participants.
b
The residential center shall include classrooms, a counseling and vocational training center, separate sleeping accommodations for male and female participants, a dining facility, outdoor drill and recreation areas, and other usages that are necessary for the efficient operation of the BOOT CAMP.
