The DC Code: § 3-1352 Establishment of Motor Vehicle Theft Prevention Commission.

Index → 3 District of Columbia Boards and Commissions. (Refs & Annos)
a
There is established a Motor Vehicle Theft Prevention Commission.
b
The purpose of the Commission is to improve and support motor vehicle theft law enforcement, prosecution, prevention, and community-education programs to reduce the incidence of motor vehicle theft in the District of Columbia. The Commission shall focus on strategies to improve public awareness/prevention, law enforcement, prosecution, and juvenile intervention.
