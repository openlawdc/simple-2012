The DC Code: § 47-1384 Construction of chapter.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

Notwithstanding any other law, the provisions of this chapter shall be liberally construed as remedial legislation to encourage the foreclosure of the right of redemption by suits in the Superior Court and for the decreeing of marketable titles to real property sold by the Mayor.
