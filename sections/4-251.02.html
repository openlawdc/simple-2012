The DC Code: § 4-251.02 Establishment of program to provide subsidies for grandparent caregivers.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
No later than March 1, 2006, the Mayor shall establish a program through which eligible grandparents may receive subsidy payments for the care and custody of a child residing in their home.
b
Repealed.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 18-111, in subsec. (a), deleted "pilot" preceding "program"; and repealed subsec. (b), which had read as follows: "(b) The pilot program shall continue through September 30, 2009."
