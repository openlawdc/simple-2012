The DC Code: § 9-705 Penalty.

Index → 9 Transportation Systems. (Refs & Annos)

Any person who knowingly and willfully violates any rule or regulation prescribed under this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor, and, upon conviction thereof, shall be fined not more than $500 or imprisoned not more than 6 months, or both.
