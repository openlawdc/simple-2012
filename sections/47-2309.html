The DC Code: § 47-2309 Fuel exported from District of Columbia exempted from taxation.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

No tax on motor vehicle fuels exported or sold for exportation from the District of Columbia to any other jurisdiction or nation shall be imposed.
