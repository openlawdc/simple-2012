The DC Code: § 4-635 Program implementation.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall make the Program available to eligible individuals by January 1, 2010.
b
To meet the deadline set forth in subsection (a) of this section, the Mayor is authorized to enter into a contract with one or more health insurers to administer the Program.
c
Any contract entered into pursuant to this section shall require annual reporting of clinical-quality measurements and utilization data to the Mayor.
