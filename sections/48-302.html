The DC Code: § 48-302 Authority of Mayor.

Index → 48 Foods and Drugs. (Refs & Annos)

Nothing in this chapter shall restrict the authority of the Mayor of the District of Columbia to inspect, condemn, denature, destroy, seize, or remove food for human consumption pursuant to § 22-2903.
