The DC Code: § 38-601 Definitions.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this subchapter:

1
"Adult student" and "minor student" mean those terms as they are defined in § 499 of the Board of Education Rules, effective July 29, 1977 (5 DCMR 2099).
2
"Certified nurse practitioner" means a registered nurse who is licensed in the United States or its territories, has had postgraduate education and training in pediatrics, adolescent medicine, or the assessment and care of school-aged children, and is certified as a nurse practitioner by the American Nurses' Association, the National Board of Pediatric Nurse Practitioners and Associates, or any other certifying organization acceptable to the Mayor.
3
"District" means the District of Columbia.
4
"Physician" means an individual who is licensed to practice medicine in the United States or its territories and has had postgraduate education or training in pediatrics or adolescent medicine.
