The DC Code: § 44-951.11 Procurement law inapplicable.

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
Chapter 3A of Title 2 and its implementing regulations shall not apply to the Corporation; except, that the Corporation shall be required to comply with the requirements regarding multiyear contracts and contracts in excess of $1 million during a 12-month period pursuant to §§ 1-204.51 and 2-352.02.
b
Procurement policies employed by the United Medical Center on the day prior to September 14, 2011, shall continue until the Corporation develops new procurement policies.
