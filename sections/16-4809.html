§ 16-4809 Challenging a standby guardianship designation.

a
An individual entitled to notice under §§ 16-4501 through 16-4524 (the Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction Act) may challenge a standby guardianship designation order by initiating a child custody proceeding in the Superior Court of the District of Columbia or in any court that could exercise jurisdiction in accordance with §§ 16-4501 through 16-4524.
b
An order of custody issued by a court of competent jurisdiction supersedes any designation of standby guardianship.
