The DC Code: § 25-407 Application for brew pub permit.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)

The application for issuance or renewal of a brew pub permit shall include:

1
A copy of the applicant's restaurant or tavern license, or a copy of the pending application for a license; and
2
A map showing the relation of the restaurant or tavern to the premises to be used to brew malt beverages.
