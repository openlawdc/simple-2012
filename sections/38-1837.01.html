The DC Code: § 38-1837.01 Establishment.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
There is established a Public Charter School Facilities Allotment Task Force ("Task Force"). The Task Force shall:
1
Consult with:
A
Public charter schools;
B
The Council;
C
Relevant District government agencies; and
D
Banking, or other financial, professionals to determine the financial implications of any changes to the current uniform per student formula for the public charter schools facilities allotment.
2
Conduct a comprehensive analysis of facilities expenditures among public charter schools, including the allowable facilities expenditures recommended by the Mayor, and identify additional factors bearing on expenditures, if any, for consideration;
3
Develop recommendations for a cost-based allocation formula for the public charter schools facilities allotment; and
4
Identify cost-saving strategies and measures to ensure that public charter schools facilities allotment funds are used exclusively on public charter school facilities.
b
The Task Force shall submit to the Council its analysis and recommendations, including its recommendation for a cost-based allocation formula for the public charter schools facilities allotment, by November 30, 2009.
c
The Task Force shall be disbanded by no later than December 31, 2009.
