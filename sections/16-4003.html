The DC Code: § 16-4003 Applicability.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

This chapter applies to a collaborative law participation agreement that meets the requirements of § 16-4004 signed on or after the effective date of this chapter.
