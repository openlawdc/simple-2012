The DC Code: § 24-134 Authorization of appropriations.

Index → 24 Prisoners and Their Treatment. (Refs & Annos)

There are authorized to be appropriated in each fiscal year such sums as may be necessary for the following:

1
District of Columbia Pretrial Services Agency.
2
Supervision of offenders on probation, parole, or supervised release for offenses under the District of Columbia Official Code.
3
Operation of the parole system for offenders convicted of offenses under the District of Columbia Official Code.
4
Operation of the Trusteeship described in § 24-132.
