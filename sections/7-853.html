The DC Code: § 7-853 Health benefit plan reimbursement.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

All health insurer health benefit plans shall reimburse for newborn hearing screenings conducted under this subchapter.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For D.C. Law 13-276, see notes following § 7-851. Miscellaneous Notes Section 7(b) of D.C. Law 13-276 provides: "(b) Section 4 shall apply to all health benefit plans issued or reissued beginning on the first day of the month 120 days following the effective date of this act."
