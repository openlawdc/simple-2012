The DC Code: § 44-109.01 Responsibilities of the ALR personnel in medication management.

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

An ALA shall ensure that an initial assessment identifies whether a resident:

1
Is capable of self-administering his or her own medications;
2
Is capable of self-administering his or her own medication, but requires a reminder to take medications or requires physical assistance with opening and removing medications from the container, or both; or
3
Requires that medications be administered by a TME or a licensed nurse.
