The DC Code: § 16-2101 Definition of joint and several contracts.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of action thereon, a contract or obligation entered into by two or more persons, whether:

is deemed to be joint and several.

1
the persons are partners or joint contractors;
2
the contract is under seal or not;
3
it is written or verbal; or
4
it is expressed to be joint and several or not --
