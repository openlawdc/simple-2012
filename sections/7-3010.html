§ 7-3010 Quality assurance for treatment providers.

Prior to implementing the Program, APRA shall:

1
Establish a Quality Assurance Division staffed with personnel to monitor the performance and quality of services of treatment providers;
2
Train quality assurance staff on the tools and protocols used to effectively monitor treatment providers; and
3
Develop policies, procedures, and instruments to measure qualitative and quantitative outcomes of treatment services.
