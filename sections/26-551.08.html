The DC Code: § 26-551.08 Organization and operation of the Board.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Board shall be organized, shall operate, and may establish committees under such rules and bylaws as the Board establishes.
b
The Board shall meet at least twice a year and at the call of the Commissioner.
