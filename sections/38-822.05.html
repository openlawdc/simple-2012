The DC Code: § 38-822.05 Public disclosure.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
Food service providers shall provide the following information to public schools, public charter schools, and participating private schools:
1
The menu for each breakfast and lunch meal served;
2
The nutritional content of each menu item;
3
The ingredients for each menu item if requested by parents and legal guardians; and
4
The location where fruits and vegetables served in schools are grown and processed and whether growers are engaged in sustainable agriculture practices.
b
(1) Public schools, public charter schools, and participating private schools shall provide the information provided to them under subsection (a) of this section:
A
In the school's office;
B
Online, if the school has a website; and
C
To parents and legal guardians upon request.
2
Public schools, public charter schools, and participating private schools shall inform families that vegetarian food options and milk alternatives are available upon request.
c
This section shall apply as of January 1, 2012.
