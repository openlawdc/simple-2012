Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 18-293, the "800 Kenilworth Avenue Northeast Redevelopment Project Real Property Limited Tax Abatement Assistance Act of 2010", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 18-828, which was referred to the Committee on Finance and Revenue. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on October 5, 2010, and November 9, 2010, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on November 23, 2010, it was assigned Act No. 18-614 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 18-293 became effective on March 8, 2011.
