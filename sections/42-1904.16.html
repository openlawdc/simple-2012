§ 42-1904.16 Judicial review of mayoral actions.

Proceedings for judicial review of mayoral actions shall be subject to and be in accordance with the District of Columbia Administrative Procedure Act (§ 2-501 et seq.) applicable to "rule-making"; provided, however, that review of mayoral actions pursuant to § 42-1904.06 shall be subject to provisions applicable to "contested cases."
