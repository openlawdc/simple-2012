The DC Code: § 31-3135 Claims payment report.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

A health insurer shall include with its annual report filed with the Commissioner a claims payment report to include the:

1
Number of claims received in the previous calendar year;
2
Number of claims denied in the previous calendar year;
3
Number of claims paid:
A
In the previous calendar year;
B
In 30 days;
C
In 60 days;
D
In 120 days; and
E
In more than 120 days; and
4
Average number of days to pay a claim submitted in the previous calendar year.
