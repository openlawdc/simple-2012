§ 27-103 Criminal proceedings.

a
The recovery of damages from the alleged offender shall not prohibit criminal prosecution of the alleged offender.
b
The recovery of civil damages by a merchant or a finding of liability under this chapter shall not be admissible in a criminal proceeding.
c
A conviction or plea of guilty of fraud, shoplifting, or theft is not a prerequisite to the maintenance of a civil action authorized by this chapter.
