The DC Code: § 10-1904 Transfer to home-less assistance provider.

Index → 10 Parks, Public Buildings, Grounds, and Space. (Refs & Annos)

Notwithstanding Chapter 8 of this title, the Mayor is authorized to transfer the subject real property to the applicable homeless-assistance provider in accordance with the terms of the applicable Legally Binding Agreement.
