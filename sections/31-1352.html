§ 31-1352 Ancillary summary proceedings.

The Commissioner, in his or her sole discretion, may institute proceedings under §§ 31-1308 and 31-1309 at the request of the commissioner or other appropriate insurance official of the domiciliary state of any foreign or alien insurer having property located in the District.
