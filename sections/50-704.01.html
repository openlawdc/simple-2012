The DC Code: § 50-704.01 Report by Mayor.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)

The Mayor, on or before March 1 of each year, shall submit to the Council a report detailing the following:

a
The total number of alternative-fuel vehicles purchased by the District in the previous fiscal year, by agency;
b
The total number of alternative-fuel vehicles owned by the District, by agency;
c
The total number of vehicles owned by the District, by agency;
d
The percentage of alternative-fuel vehicles, by agency;
e
A plan to purchase additional alternative-fuel vehicles in the upcoming fiscal year and subsequent fiscal years; and
f
A plan to comply with the purchase requirements mandated by this chapter.
