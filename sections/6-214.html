§ 6-214 Social services teams in public housing.

a
The Authority may, subject to availability of funds, develop a plan for the provision of social services within the Housing Properties which it owns, operates, or manages, and establish social service teams to implement that plan.
b
Nothing in this section shall be construed to prohibit other District or United States government departments and agencies from providing social services to public housing residents.
