§ 51-136 Disclosure of information pertaining to domestic violence claimant.

The release of information pertaining to a domestic violence claimant, in addition to the requirements of § 51-113, shall require that:

1
The Director notify the claimant prior to the release of any information;
2
The Director shall take reasonable actions to prevent the unnecessary disclosure of personal identifiers, such as the claimant's address, from information otherwise required to be disclosed by law; and
3
Further dissemination of the information released shall be prohibited.
