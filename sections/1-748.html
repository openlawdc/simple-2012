The DC Code: § 1-748 Claims procedure.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

In accordance with regulations issued by the Board, the Board shall provide to any participant or beneficiary who has a claim for benefits under a retirement program denied:

1
Adequate written notice of such denial, setting forth the specific reasons for such denial in a manner calculated to be understood by such participant or beneficiary; and
2
A reasonable opportunity for a full and fair review of the decision denying such claim.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 1-748. 1973 Ed., § 1-1848. Effect of Amendments D.C.
