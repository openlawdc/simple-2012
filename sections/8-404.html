The DC Code: § 8-404 Registered employees.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)
a
No person, not licensed pursuant to this chapter and not acting as a private applicator, shall administer any pesticide unless he is registered with the Mayor and is acting under the direct supervision of a licensed applicator.
b
Application for registration shall be made in writing on a form prescribed by the Mayor and the registration shall be valid for the time period prescribed by the Mayor. The Mayor shall provide for the issuance of appropriate credentials for all registrants.
