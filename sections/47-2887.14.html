§ 47-2887.14 Criminal penalties; prosecution by Corporation Counsel.

An athlete agent who violates § 47-2887.13 is guilty of a misdemeanor and, upon conviction, is punishable by maximum fine of $10,000 or imprisonment of 6 months, or both. Violations shall be prosecuted by the Attorney General for the District of Columbia in the name of the District of Columbia.
