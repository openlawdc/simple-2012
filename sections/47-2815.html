The DC Code: § 47-2815 Pyroxylin.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)
a
Owners or managers of establishments where pyroxylin is kept or stored for painting or spraying shall pay a license fee of $50 per annum. No license shall issue hereunder without the approval of the Fire Marshal of the District of Columbia.
b
Any license issued pursuant to this section shall be issued as an Environmental Materials endorsement to a basic business license under the basic business license system as set forth in subchapter I-A of this chapter.
