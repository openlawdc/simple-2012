§ 29-305.41 Voting agreements.

a
Two or more shareholders may provide for the manner in which they will vote their shares by signing an agreement for that purpose. A voting agreement created under this section shall not be subject to § 29-305.40.
b
A voting agreement created under this section shall be specifically enforceable.
