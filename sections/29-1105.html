§ 29-1105 Legal entity; duration; powers.

a
An unincorporated nonprofit association shall be a legal entity distinct from its members and managers.
b
An unincorporated nonprofit association shall have perpetual duration unless the governing principles specify otherwise.
c
An unincorporated nonprofit association shall have the same powers as an individual to do all things necessary or convenient to carry on its purposes.
d
An unincorporated nonprofit association may engage in profit-making activities, but profits from any activities shall be used or set aside for the association's nonprofit purposes.
