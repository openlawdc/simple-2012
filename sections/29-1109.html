The DC Code: § 29-1109 Assertion and defense of claims.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
An unincorporated nonprofit association shall have the capacity to sue and be sued in its own name.
b
A member or a manager may assert a claim the member or manager has against an unincorporated nonprofit association. An association may assert a claim it has against a member or a manager.
