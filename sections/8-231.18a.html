§ 8-231.18a Enforcement of housing code regulations.

The presence of loose or peeling paint in residential premises in violation of the housing code regulations codified in Title 14 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations which constitutes a lead-based paint hazard under this chapter, shall be enforced by the Mayor according to the provisions of this chapter.
