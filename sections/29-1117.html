The DC Code: § 29-1117 Membership interest not transferable.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

Except as otherwise provided in the governing principles, a member's interest or any right under the governing principles shall not be transferable.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 21 of the Uniform Unincorporated Nonprofit Association Act (2008 Act) .
