§ 29-1012.12 Statement of termination.

a
A dissolved limited cooperative association that has completed winding up may deliver to the Mayor for filing a statement of termination that states:
1
The name of the association;
2
The date of filing of its initial articles of organization; and
3
That the association is terminated.
b
The filing of a statement of termination shall not itself terminate the limited cooperative association.
