The DC Code: § 1-321.02 Applicability of this subchapter.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

This subchapter shall apply to the boards, commissions, and committees and the members thereof, respectively, established pursuant to the following sections: 1-301.01, 3-1601 to 3-1631, 3-1701 to 3-1719, 3-2001 to 3-2028, 3-2101 to 3- 2132, 3-2901 to 3-2943, 3-2301.01 to 3-2301.10, 3-2401 to 3-2422, 3-2501 to 3- 2508, 3-2601 to 3-2619, 47-2886.01 to 47-2886.18, 3-2701 to 3-2707, 42-1701 to 42-1709, and 47-2801 to 47-2849.
