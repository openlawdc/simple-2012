§ 4-754.23 Additional standards for providers of low barrier shelter.

In addition to the requirements in §§ 4-754.21 and 4-754.22, providers of low barrier shelter shall provide:

1
Case management services with an appropriately trained, qualified, and supervised case manager, which shall include the development of a service plan;
2
Hot shower facilities; and
3
Personal hygiene supplies.
