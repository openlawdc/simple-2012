The DC Code: § 1-1162.20 Reports.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Director of Government Ethics shall produce a quarterly report detailing:
1
The posture of each complaint it received, including whether an investigation was initiated, is ongoing, or has concluded;
2
The referrals made to and from the Ethics Board;
3
Fines and penalties imposed by the Ethics Board;
4
Allegations dismissed by the Ethics Board; and
5
Other action taken with regard to an allegation of a violation of the Code of Conduct.
b
The quarterly report shall be posted online.
