The DC Code: § 28-2101 Form of assignment.

Index → 28 Commercial Instruments and Transactions. (Refs & Annos)

In a voluntary assignment for the benefit of creditors, the debtor shall annex to the assignment (1) an inventory, under oath or affirmation, of his estate, real and personal, according to the best of his knowledge, (2) a list of his creditors, their respective residences and places of business, if known, and (3) the amounts of their respective demands.
