§ 31-2412 Temporary Motor Vehicle Insurance Review Commission.   [Expired]

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 35-2112. Miscellaneous Notes Expiration of Temporary Motor Vehicle Insurance Review Commission: Pursuant to subsection (i) of former § 35-2112 [1981 Ed.], the Temporary Motor Vehicle Insurance Review Commission was to expire 3 years after March 4, 1986. The Temporary Motor Vehicle Insurance Review Commission is deemed to have expired on March 4, 1989. DC CODE § 31-2412 Current through December 11, 2012
