The DC Code: § 44-110.05 Accessibility

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

An ALR that provides services for residents who use wheelchairs, shall insure that:

1
Doorways and hallways provide a clear opening of at least 32 inches; and
2
Thresholds exceeding 1/2 inch are modified to provide a 1:12 maximum slope.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 19-169 substituted "residents who use wheelchairs" for "wheelchair-bound residents". Legislative History of Laws For Law 13-127, see notes following § 44-101.01.
