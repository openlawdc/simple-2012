The DC Code: § 34-1264.05 Transition provisions.

Index → 34 Public Utilities. (Refs & Annos)
a
All appointments, rules, regulations, orders, administrative issuances, obligations, determinations, agreements, and understandings made, established, issued, promulgated, or entered into under the authority of subchapter I of this chapter, shall remain in effect until amended, modified, superseded, or repealed.
b
The Public Access Corporation established in accordance with § 34-1229, may continue to serve as the Public Access Corporation required by § 34- 1253.02(a); provided, that the Corporation shall amend its bylaws to be consistent with the provisions of this subchapter.
c
The funds in the special account established by § 34-1207.01, shall be transferred to the special account established by § 34-1252.03.
