§ 24-401.01 Board of Parole--Creation; term of members.

Abolished.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 24-201.1. Legislative History of Laws Law 7-103, the "District of Columbia Board of Parole Amendment Act of 1987," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 7-133, which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Nov. 10, 1987 and Nov. 24, 1987, respectively. Deemed approved without the signature of the Mayor on December 14, 1987, it was assigned Act No. 7-122 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
