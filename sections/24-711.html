The DC Code: § 24-711 Patient not deemed a criminal.

Index → 24 Prisoners and Their Treatment. (Refs & Annos)

The patient in any proceedings under §§ 24-701 to 24-711 shall not be deemed a criminal and the commitment of any such patient shall not be deemed a conviction.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 24-611. 1973 Ed., § 24-611. DC CODE § 24-711 Current through December 11, 2012
