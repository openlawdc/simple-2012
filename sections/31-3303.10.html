The DC Code: § 31-3303.10 Exclusions.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)

The provisions of this subchapter shall not apply to:

1
Any group health benefit plan for any plan year if, on the first day of such plan year, such plan has less than 2 participants who are current employees; or
2
Any health benefit plan for any of the excepted benefits.
