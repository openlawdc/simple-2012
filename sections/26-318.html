§ 26-318 Books, accounts, and other records of licensee.

Each licensee under this chapter shall keep and use such books, accounts, and other records as the Superintendent shall require to carry into effect the provisions of this chapter and any rules and regulations issued by the Superintendent under this chapter. Every licensee shall preserve such books, accounts, and records for at least 3 years.
