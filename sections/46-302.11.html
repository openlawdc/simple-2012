The DC Code: § 46-302.11 Continuing, exclusive jurisdiction to modify spousal-support order.

Index → 46 Domestic Relations. (Refs & Annos)
a
A tribunal of the District issuing a spousal-support order consistent with the law of the District has continuing, exclusive jurisdiction to modify the spousal-support order throughout the existence of the support obligation.
b
A tribunal of the District may not modify a spousal-support order issued by a tribunal of another state having continuing, exclusive jurisdiction over that order under the law of that state.
c
A tribunal of the District that has continuing, exclusive jurisdiction over a spousal-support order may serve as:
1
An initiating tribunal to request a tribunal of another state to enforce the spousal-support order issued in the District; or
2
A responding tribunal to enforce or modify its own spousal-support order.
