§ 10-503.26 Definitions.

As used in this part:

1
The term "Capitol Buildings" means the United States Capitol, the Senate and House Office Buildings and garages, the Capitol Power Plant, all subways and enclosed passages connecting 2 or more of such structures, and the real property underlying and enclosed by any such structure.
2
The term "firearm" shall have the same meaning as when used in § 901(3) of Title 15, United States Code.
3
The term "dangerous weapon" includes all articles enumerated in § 22- 4514(a) and also any device designed to expel or hurl a projectile capable of causing injury to persons or property, daggers, dirks, stilettoes, and knives having blades over 3 inches in length.
4
The term "explosive" shall have the same meaning as when used in § 121(1) of Title 50, United States Code.
5
The term "act of physical violence" means any act involving:
A
An assault or any other infliction or threat of infliction of death or bodily harm upon any individual; or
B
Damage to or destruction of any real property or personal property.
