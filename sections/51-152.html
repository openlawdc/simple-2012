Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 2001 Ed., § 51-102.01 Legislative History of Laws Law 5-3, the "District of Columbia Unemployment Compensation Act of 1983," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 5-57, which was referred to the Committee on Housing and Economic Development. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 1, 1983. Signed by the Mayor on March 15, 1983, it was assigned Act No. 5-13 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
