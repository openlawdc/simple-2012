Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 15-298, the "Free Clinic Assistance Program Extension Amendment Act of 2004", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 15-915, which was referred to the Committee on Human Services. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on November 9, 2004, and December 7, 2004, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on December 29, 2004, it was assigned Act No. 15-694 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 15-298 became effective on April 8, 2005. Miscellaneous Notes Repeal of Part B: Section 16 of D.C. Law 17-196 provides: "The Free Clinic Assistance Program Act of 1986, effective September 23, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-155; D.C. Official Code § 1-307.21 et seq.), is repealed as of the date when the Agency becomes operational." DC CODE § 1-307.23a Current through December 11, 2012
