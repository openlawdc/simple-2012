The DC Code: § 1-632.08 Rules of construction.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

In accordance with the express terms of this chapter, the following rules of construction will apply in the interpretation of provisions in apparent conflict:

1
Subchapter II will govern conflicting provisions; and
2
A parenthetical limitation, upon provisions of a section or subchapter preceding it, shall limit the scope of the section or subchapter to the parenthetical provision.
