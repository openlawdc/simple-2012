§ 34-1437 Renewable electricity tracking system.

a
The Commission shall select a market-based renewable electricity tracking system to facilitate the creation and transfer of renewable energy credits.
b
The Commission may designate the Energy Office to administer the electricity tracking system. The Commission or the Energy Office may contract with a for-profit or a nonprofit entity to assist in the administration of the electricity tracking system required under this section.
c
To the extent practicable, the tracking system shall be the generation attributes tracking system developed by PJM Interconnection.
