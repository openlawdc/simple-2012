The DC Code: § 42-503 Fee simple estates--Absolute or qualified.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

An estate in fee simple may be either absolute or qualified, as to one and his heirs during an existing condition of things of uncertain duration.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-203. 1973 Ed., § 45-803. DC CODE § 42-503 Current through December 11, 2012
