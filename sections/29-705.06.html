The DC Code: § 29-705.06 Distribution in kind.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A partner shall not have a right to demand or receive any distribution from a limited partnership in any form other than cash. Subject to § 29-708. 09(b), a limited partnership may distribute an asset in kind to the extent each partner receives a percentage of the asset equal to the partner's share of distributions.
