The DC Code: § 21-2402.02 Exclusive basis.

Index → 21 Fiduciary Relations and Persons with Mental Illness. (Refs & Annos)

This subchapter provides the exclusive jurisdictional basis for the Superior Court to appoint a guardian or issue a protective order for an adult.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For Law 17-270, see notes following § 21-2401.01. Uniform Law This section is based upon § 202 of the Uniform Adult Guardianship and Protective Proceedings Jurisdiction Act.
