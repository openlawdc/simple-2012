The DC Code: § 2-218.71 Triennial review of program and subchapter.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
b
The Department and the Commission shall review the findings in the triennial report and the goals, intents, and purposes of this subchapter. The Department shall, and the Commission may, transmit to the Council and the Mayor a report setting forth any recommended amendments to this subchapter.
