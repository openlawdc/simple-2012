§ 39-501.04 Motion picture and television production permits.

a
The Mayor may issue a permit for the occupation of the public space for motion picture, television, and other media productions ("film permit") pursuant to § 10-1141.03.
b
The Mayor may impose a one-time fee for processing of the film permit application in the amount of $30 per production.
c
The film permit fee shall be $150 per day per location to occupy public space or a public right-of-way.
d
The Mayor may periodically revise the schedule of fees by rulemaking.
e
The fees received by the Mayor from applications for and issuance of the film permits shall be deposited into the special account established by § 39-501.05.
f
No permit may be transferred from one location to another.
