§ 48-831.11 Eligibility procedures.

The Department shall:

1
Establish simplified procedures for determining eligibility and issuing AccessRx enrollment cards to qualified residents;
2
Undertake outreach efforts to build public awareness of AccessRx and maximize enrollment of qualified residents; and
3
Adjust the requirements and terms of AccessRx to accommodate any new federally funded prescription drug program.
