§ 22-1301 Affrays.

Whoever is convicted of an affray in the District shall be fined not more than $1,000 or imprisoned not more than 180 days, or both.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 22-1101. 1973 Ed., § 22-1101. Emergency Act Amendments For temporary amendment of section, see § 107 of the Omnibus Criminal Justice Reform Emergency Amendment Act of 1994 (D.C. Act 10-255, June 22, 1994, 41 DCR 4286). The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 29, 1994, and April 12, 1994, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on May 4, 1994, it was assigned Act No. 10-238 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 10-151 became effective on August 20, 1994.
