The DC Code: § 2-591 Short title.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

This subchapter may be cited as the "Open Government Office Act".

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 18-350, the "Open Meetings Amendment Act of 2010", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 18-716, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations and the Environment. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 7, 2010, and December 21, 2010, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on January 19, 2011, it was assigned Act No. 18-700 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 18-350 became effective on March 31, 2011.
