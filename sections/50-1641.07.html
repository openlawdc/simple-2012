§ 50-1641.07 Rules; Council review.

a
Within 90 days of April 8, 2011, the Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of this subchapter.
b
All rules promulgated under this subchapter shall be submitted to the Council for review and approval.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 18-365 substituted "Within 90 days of April 8, 2011" for "Within 90 days of February 2, 2008".
