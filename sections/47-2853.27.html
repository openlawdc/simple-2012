The DC Code: § 47-2853.27 Fines and penalties; criminal violations.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)
a
Any person who violates any provision of this subchapter shall, upon conviction, be subject to imprisonment not to exceed one year, a fine not to exceed $10,000, or both.
b
Any person who has been previously convicted under this subchapter shall, upon conviction, be subject to imprisonment not to exceed one year, a fine not to exceed $25,000, or both.
