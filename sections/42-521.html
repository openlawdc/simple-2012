The DC Code: § 42-521 Estates from month to month or from quarter to quarter.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

An estate may be from month to month or from quarter to quarter, or, as otherwise expressed, it may be by the month or by the quarter, if so expressed in writing.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-221. 1973 Ed., § 45-821. DC CODE § 42-521 Current through December 11, 2012
