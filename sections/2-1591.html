This subchapter may be cited as the "Interagency Collaboration and Services Integration Commission Establishment Act of 2007".

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 17-9, the "Public Education Reform Amendment Act of 2007", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 17-1, which was referred to Committee of the Whole. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on April 3, 2007, and April 19, 2007, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on April 23, 2007, it was assigned Act No. 17-38 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 17-9 became effective on June 12, 2007. Miscellaneous Notes Applicability: Section 508 of Law 17-9 provides that this title [this subchapter] shall apply upon Congressional enactment of Title IX.
