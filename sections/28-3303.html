§ 28-3303 Usury defined.

If a person or corporation contracts in the District,

This section does not affect sections 26-901 to 26-912.

1
verbally, to pay a greater rate of interest than 6% per annum, or
2
in writing, to pay a greater rate than is permitted under section 28- 3301, 28-3308, under Chapter 36 of this subtitle, or under § 26-301 et seq., the creditor shall forfeit the whole of the interest so contracted to be received.
