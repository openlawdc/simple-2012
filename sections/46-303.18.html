The DC Code: § 46-303.18 Assistance with discovery.

Index → 46 Domestic Relations. (Refs & Annos)

A tribunal of the District may:

1
Request a tribunal of another state to assist in obtaining discovery; and
2
Upon request, compel a person over whom it has jurisdiction to respond to a discovery order issued by a tribunal of another state.
