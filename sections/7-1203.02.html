§ 7-1203.02 Disclosures under law.

Mental health information may be disclosed by a mental health professional or mental health facility where necessary and, to the extent necessary:

1
To meet the requirements of § 21-586 (concerning financial responsibility for the care of hospitalized persons);
2
To meet the compulsory reporting provisions of District or federal law that seek to promote human health and safety, including §4-1371.12; or
3
For the purposes of and in accordance with Chapter 2A of this title.
