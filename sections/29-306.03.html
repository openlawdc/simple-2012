The DC Code: § 29-306.03 Number and election of directors.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
A board of directors shall consist of one or more individuals, with the number specified in or fixed in accordance with the articles of incorporation or bylaws.
b
The number of directors may be increased or decreased by amendment to, or in the manner provided in, the articles of incorporation or the bylaws.
c
Directors shall be elected at the 1st annual shareholders' meeting and at each annual meeting thereafter unless their terms are staggered under § 29- 306.06.
