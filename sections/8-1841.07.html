§ 8-1841.07 Care of animal.

A commercial guard dog shall not be maintained on any premises unless the dog is provided:

1
Full access to an enclosed shelter sufficient to protect the dog from wind, rain, excessive heat or cold, and disease; and
2
Continuous access to sufficient food and water.
