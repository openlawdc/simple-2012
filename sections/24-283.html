The DC Code: § 24-283 Uses of Inmate Welfare Fund.

Index → 24 Prisoners and Their Treatment. (Refs & Annos)

The Fund shall be used for the following purposes, in order of priority:

1
To stock the commissaries of District correctional facilities;
2
To repay the initial appropriation used to finance the Fund; and
3
To provide goods and services that benefit the general inmate population at District correctional facilities, as determined by the Inmate Welfare Fund Committee established in § 24-284.
