§ 31-5412 Examination of the Association; annual report.

a
The Association shall be subject to examination and regulation by the Mayor.
b
The Board of Directors shall submit to the Mayor each year, not later than 120 days after the end of the Association's fiscal year, a financial report in a form approved by the Mayor and a report of its activities during the preceding fiscal year.
