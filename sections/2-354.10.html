The DC Code: § 2-354.10 General Services Administration schedules.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)
a
The CPO may procure goods or services through a General Services Administration schedule pursuant to 40 U.S.C. § 502(a)(3) and 40 U.S.C. § 602(c).
b
The CPO shall ensure that the price of any contract entered into under subsection (a) of this section is reasonable.
