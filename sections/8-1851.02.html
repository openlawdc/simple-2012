The DC Code: § 8-1851.02 Care of classroom animals.

Index → 8 Environmental and Animal Control and Protection (Refs & Annos)
a
Animals kept in schools shall be provided sufficient food and water, be cared for in a safe and humane manner, and remain in schools during holidays only if provided adequate care.
b
Animals no longer needed in the classroom should be adopted out to a suitable home or given to a local humane organization for adoption.
