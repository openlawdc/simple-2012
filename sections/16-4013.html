§ 16-4013 Standards of professional responsibility and mandatory reporting not affected.

This chapter does not affect:

1
The professional responsibility obligations and standards applicable to a lawyer or other licensed professional; or
2
The obligation of a person to report abuse, neglect, abandonment, or exploitation of a child or adult under the law of the District of Columbia.
