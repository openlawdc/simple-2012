The DC Code: § 1-606.05 Authority of Council to issue rules mandated by § 1-606.04(a).

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)

The officers of the Council of the District of Columbia may issue rules, subject to approval by the Council of the District of Columbia, concerning review of central staff employee personnel appeals as mandated in § 1- 606.04(a).

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 1-606.5. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 30, 1981 and July 14, 1981, respectively.
