The DC Code: § 29-605.01 Partner not co-owner of partnership property.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A partner shall not be a co-owner of partnership property and has no interest in partnership property which can be transferred, either voluntarily or involuntarily.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 41-155.1. 2001 Ed., § 33-105.01. Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01.
