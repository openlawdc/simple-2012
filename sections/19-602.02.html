The DC Code: § 19-602.02 Limitation on scope of subchapter.

Index → 19 Descent, Distribution, and Trusts. (Refs & Annos)

This subchapter does not apply to:

1
An account established for a partnership, joint venture, or other organization for a business purpose;
2
An account controlled by one or more persons as an agent or trustee for a corporation, unincorporated association, or charitable or civic organization; or
3
A fiduciary or trust account in which the relationship is established other than by the terms of the account.
