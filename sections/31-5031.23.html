The DC Code: § 31-5031.23 Rules; orders.

Index → 31 Insurance and Securities. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Commissioner, through the Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, may issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.
b
The Commissioner may issue orders to implement the provisions of this chapter.
