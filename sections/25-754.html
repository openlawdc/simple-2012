§ 25-754 Restrictions on storage of beverages.

a
Alcoholic beverages shall not be manufactured, kept for sale, or sold by any licensee other than at the licensed establishment; provided, that the Board may permit the storing of beverages upon premises other than the licensed establishment under the following classes of licenses:
1
Manufacturer's license;
2
Wholesaler's license;
3
Off-premises retailer's license, class A;
4
Common carrier license, class C or D; and
5
Caterer's license.
b
A licensee may not store alcoholic beverages upon premises outside the District.
