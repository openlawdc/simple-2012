§ 21-103 Appointment of guardians of the person by court; limitation of number of wards.

a
When an infant has neither a natural nor testamentary guardian, a guardian of the person may be appointed by the Probate Court in its own discretion or on the application of a next friend of the infant.
b
Only trust companies may act as guardian of the person for more than five infants at one time, unless the infants are members of one family.
