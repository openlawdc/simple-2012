The DC Code: § 26-1155.01 Applicability.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
Subchapters I through III of this chapter shall apply 60 days after the effective date of the regulations promulgated by the Mayor pursuant to this chapter.
b
The provisions of this chapter shall be interpreted and applied to the fullest extent practical in a manner consistent with applicable federal laws and regulations. Nothing in this chapter is intended to preempt federal laws and regulations.
