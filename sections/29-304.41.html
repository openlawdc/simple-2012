§ 29-304.41 Corporation's acquisition of its own shares.

a
A corporation may acquire its own shares and shares so acquired shall constitute authorized but unissued shares.
b
If the articles of incorporation prohibit the reissue of the acquired shares, the number of authorized shares shall be reduced by the number of shares acquired.
