§ 29-1202.02 Amendment or restatement of certificate of trust; statement of correction.

a
To amend its certificate of trust, a statutory trust shall deliver to the Mayor for filing an amendment, articles of conversion, or articles of merger stating the:
1
Name of the trust;
2
Date of filing of its initial certificate; and
3
Changes to the certificate.
b
A trustee that knows or has reason to know that any information in a filed certificate of trust was incorrect when the certificate was filed or has become incorrect shall promptly:
1
Cause the certificate to be amended; or
2
Deliver to the Mayor for filing a statement of correction.
c
A restated certificate of trust shall be delivered to the Mayor for filing in the same manner as an amendment.
