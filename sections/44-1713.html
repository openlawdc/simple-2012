The DC Code: § 44-1713 Severability.

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

If any provision of this chapter, or the application thereof to any persons or circumstances, is held invalid, the remainder of the chapter, and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances, shall not be affected thereby.
