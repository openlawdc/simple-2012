The DC Code: § 44-637 Rules.

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

The Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, may issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.

Historical and Statutory

Temporary Addition of Section Section 1108 of D.C. Law 18-222 added a section to read as follows: "Sec. 1108. Rules. "The Mayor, pursuant to Title I of the District of Columbia Administrative Procedure Act, approved October 21, 1968 (82 Stat. 1204; D.C. Official Code § 2-501 et seq.), may issue rules to implement the provisions of this act." Section 2002(b) of D.C. Law 18-222 provides that the act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
