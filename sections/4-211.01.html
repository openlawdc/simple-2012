The DC Code: § 4-211.01 Home repairs--Run-down premises.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)

The Mayor may authorize an expenditure for repairs to a home which a recipient of TANF, POWER, or GAC owns or is buying, when there has been no assignment or transfer to the District of such property, if:

1
A determination has been made that:
A
The home is so defective that continued occupancy is not warranted;
B
Unless repairs are made the recipient would have to move to rental quarters; and
C
The rental cost of quarters for the recipient and his family over a period of 2 years would exceed the cost of repairs needed to make the home habitable together with other costs attributable to continued occupancy of the home; and
2
There has been no expenditure for repairs prior to the determination described in paragraph (1) of this section.
