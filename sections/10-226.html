The DC Code: § 10-226 Transfer of equipment, etc., of Community Center and Playgrounds Department.

Index → 10 Parks, Public Buildings, Grounds, and Space. (Refs & Annos)

All equipment, machinery, supplies, and materials of the Community Center and Playgrounds Department shall, on the effective date of this chapter, be transferred to the Board.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 8-226. 1973 Ed., § 8-219. References in Text The words "effective date of this chapter", appearing in this section, mean 30 days from the date of its approval. Miscellaneous Notes Recreation Board abolished: See Historical and Statutory Notes following § 10- 201. DC CODE § 10-226 Current through December 11, 2012
