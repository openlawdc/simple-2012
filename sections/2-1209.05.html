§ 2-1209.05 Services.

a
Selected basic services shall be included in the rent for the business incubator; other services shall be paid for on an as-used basis.
b
The only free services shall be those ordinarily available free of charge from participating providers.
c
Services available elsewhere shall not be duplicated; all existing management support and services programs in the District, including financial services, shall be utilized.
d
Both public and private resources may be made available to tenant businesses.
