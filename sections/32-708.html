The DC Code: § 32-708 Recordkeeping requirements.

Index → 32 Labor. (Refs & Annos)
a
The Mayor shall maintain adequate records of declarations of domestic partnership, termination statements, and amendments to declarations of domestic partnership and termination statements.
b
The Mayor shall report annually to the Council on:
1
The number of domestic partnerships declared and terminated; and
2
Utilization of domestic partnership benefits by District government employees.
