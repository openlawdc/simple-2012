§ 8-108.03 Phase out of perchloroethylene in dry cleaning.

a
After January 1, 2014, no person or legal entity shall install a machine designed to use perchloroethylene as a cleaning agent for clothes or other fabrics.
b
After January 1, 2029, no person or legal entity shall use perchloroethlyene as a cleaning agent for clothes or other fabrics.
