§ 28-4501 Definition and purpose.

a
As used in this chapter, the term "person" includes an individual, corporation, business trust, partnership, business association, or any other legal entity.
b
The purpose of this chapter is to promote the unhampered freedom of commerce and industry throughout the District of Columbia by prohibiting restraints of trade and monopolistic practices.
