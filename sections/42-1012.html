The DC Code: § 42-1012 Special powers in trust.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

A special power is in trust:

1
When the disposition which it authorizes is limited to be made to any person or class of persons other than the grantee of such power;
2
When any person or class of persons other than the grantee is designated as entitled to any benefit from the disposition or change authorized by the power.
