§ 2-218.25 By-laws and internal rules.

1
The Commission may promulgate, amend, repeal, and enforce any by-laws and internal rules of operation, consistent with the provisions of this subchapter, as may be necessary or appropriate to carry out its responsibilities under this subchapter.
2
The Department may promulgate, amend, repeal, and enforce any bylaws and internal rules of operation, consistent with the provisions of this subchapter, as may be necessary or appropriate to carry out its responsibilities under this subchapter.
