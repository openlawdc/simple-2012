§ 29-106.04 Judicial review of denial of reinstatement.

a
If the Mayor denies a domestic filing entity's application for reinstatement following administrative dissolution, the Mayor shall serve the entity pursuant to § 29-104.12 with a notice in a record that explains the reason or reasons for denial.
b
An entity may seek judicial review of denial of reinstatement in the Superior Court not later than 30 days after service of the notice of denial.
