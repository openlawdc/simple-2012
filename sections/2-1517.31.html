The DC Code: § 2-1517.31 Definitions.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

For the purposes of this part, the term:

1
"Behavioral health" means a person's overall social, emotional, and psychological well-being and development.
2
"Health education standards" means the specific learning requirements related to health that the Office of the State Superintendent of Education requires students to learn at each academic level, from pre-K through 12th grade.
