The DC Code: § 42-517 Coparcenary estates abolished.

Index → 42 Real Property. (Refs & Annos)

There shall be no estate in coparcenary in the District, and where 2 or more persons inherit from an intestate they shall be tenants in common.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 45-217. 1973 Ed., § 45-817. DC CODE § 42-517 Current through December 11, 2012
