§ 1-1162.22 Additional penalties for public officials.

a
In addition to the penalties set forth in § 1-1162.21, the Ethics Board may censure a public official for a violation of the Code of Conduct that the Ethics Board finds to substantially threaten the public trust.
b
The Ethics Board may recommend in such censure that the Council suspend or remove a Councilmember's committee chairmanship, if any, committee membership, if any, or vote in any committee.
