The DC Code: § 1-621.06 Types of benefits.

Index → 1 Government Organization. (Refs & Annos)
a
The benefits provided under the health benefit plans shall include benefits for costs associated with care in a general hospital and for health services of a catastrophic nature and may include at a minimum the following benefits:
1
Hospital benefits;
2
Managed care;
3
Office visits;
4
Substance abuse;
5
Well baby care;
6
Prescription drugs;
7
Obstetrical benefits;
8
Mental health benefits; and
9
Hospice care.
b
Each contract issued under § 1-621.05 shall comply with the provisions of Chapter 31 of Title 31.
