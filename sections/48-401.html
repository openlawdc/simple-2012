§ 48-401 Definitions.

For the purposes of this chapter, the term:

1
"Food" means any substance produced from the ground for human consumption and nourishment, such as vegetables, fruits, and nuts.
2
"Urban gardens" means any vacant lot used for the growing of food, flowers, or greenery.
3
"Vacant lot" means any lot in the District of Columbia on which there is no lawful structure.
