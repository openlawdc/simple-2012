§ 31-3931.19 Captive insurance regulatory and supervision trust account.

All fees, fines, penalties, and assessments received by the Commissioner under this chapter shall be deposited in, and credited to, the account established by § 31-1202(b-1), and expended in accordance with § 31-1202(b-1).
