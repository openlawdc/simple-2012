The DC Code: § 9-1207.02 Submarine cables at drawbridge openings.

Index → 9 Transportation Systems. (Refs & Annos)

Submarine cables may be used at drawbridge openings, provided previous approval shall have been obtained from the Department of the Army.

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 7-1431. 1973 Ed., § 7-1231. DC CODE § 9-1207.02 Current through December 11, 2012
