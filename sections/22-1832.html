The DC Code: § 22-1832 Forced labor.

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)
a
It is unlawful for an individual or a business knowingly to use coercion to cause a person to provide labor or services.
b
It is unlawful for an individual or a business knowingly to place or keep any person in debt bondage.
