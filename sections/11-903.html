The DC Code: § 11-903 Composition.

Index → 11 Organization and Jurisdiction of the Courts. (Refs & Annos)

The Superior Court of the District of Columbia shall consist of a chief judge and 61 associate judges.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 11-903. 1973 Ed., § 11-903. Effect of Amendments Pub. Law 110-201 substituted "61" for "fifty-eight". DC CODE § 11-903 Current through December 11, 2012
