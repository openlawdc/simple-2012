The DC Code: § 28-4515 Uniformity.

Index → 28 Commercial Instruments and Transactions. (Refs & Annos)

It is the intent of the Council of the District of Columbia that in construing this chapter, a court of competent jurisdiction may use as a guide interpretations given by federal courts to comparable antitrust statutes.
