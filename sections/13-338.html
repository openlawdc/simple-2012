The DC Code: § 13-338 Prerequisites for order of publication.

Index → 13 Procedure Generally. (Refs & Annos)

An order for the substitution of publication for personal service may not be made until:

1
a summons for the defendant has been issued and returned "Not to be found," and
2
the nonresidence of the defendant or his absence for at least six months is proved by affidavit to the satisfaction of the court.
