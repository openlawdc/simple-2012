§ 11-1730 Reports of court personnel.

a
Judges of the courts shall furnish time and attendance records pursuant to sections 11-709 and 11-909 to the respective chief judges, with a copy to the Executive Officer.
b
All nonjudicial personnel of the courts shall furnish such reports and information to the Executive Officer as the Executive Officer shall request.
