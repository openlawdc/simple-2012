§ 1-307.62 Establishment of Opportunity Account Office.

The Mayor shall establish in the executive branch an office to be known as the Opportunity Account Office. The office shall:

1
Provide eligible families and individuals with an opportunity to establish opportunity accounts;
2
Provide that the opportunity account shall be established by an approved financial institution and administered by an approved organization;
3
Provide incentives to encourage participation in the program; and
4
Require that money deposited into an opportunity account shall be used only for approved purposes.
