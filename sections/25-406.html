The DC Code: § 25-406 Application for a solicitor's license.

Index → 25 Alcoholic Beverage Regulation (Refs & Annos)

The application for the issuance or renewal of a solicitor's license shall include:

1
The full name and home address of the applicant, if an individual;
2
The business name and address of the applicant;
3
The name, business address, and business telephone number for the vendor that the applicant represents; and
4
Written statements and evidence establishing to the satisfaction of the Board that the applicant meets all of the qualifications set forth in § 25- 301.
