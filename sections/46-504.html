§ 46-504 Effect of marriage or domestic partnership.

A premarital agreement becomes effective upon marriage or the registration of a domestic partnership under § 32-702.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 30-144. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 16-79 rewrote section which had read as follows: "A premarital agreement becomes effective upon marriage." Legislative History of Laws For legislative history of D.C. Law 11-82, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 46-501. For Law 16-79, see notes following § 46-501. Uniform Law This section is based upon § 4 of the Uniform Premarital Agreement Act. See 9C Uniform Laws Annotated, Master Edition, or ULA Database on Westlaw.
