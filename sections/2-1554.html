The DC Code: § 2-1554 Biannual assessment and funding report.

Index → 2 Government Administration. (Refs & Annos)

The Children and Youth Investment Corporation, or a successor single non-service provider, nonprofit organization, shall submit a biannual assessment and funding report to the Council, which includes:

1
A research-based needs assessment of at-risk youth, which identifies:
A
Available resources;
B
Needed resources, if any; and
C
Any gap in services; and
2
A list of funding priorities based upon the needs assessment.
