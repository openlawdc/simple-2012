The DC Code: § 16-923 Abolition of action for breach of promise, alienation of affections, and criminal conversation.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

Causes of action for breach of promise, alienation of affections, and criminal conversation are hereby abolished.
