§ 4-219.01 Authorization.

a
The Mayor shall include in his or her annual estimates of appropriations such sums as may be needed to carry out the provisions of this chapter.
b
Unobligated balances of appropriations for the Department of Human Services, established by Reorganization Plan No. 2 of 1979, are made available for the purposes of this chapter.
