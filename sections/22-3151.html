§ 22-3151 Short title.

This chapter may be cited as the "Anti-Terrorism Act of 2002".

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws Law 14-194, the "Omnibus Anti-Terrorism Act of 2002", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 14-373, which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary.  The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on April 9, 2002, and May 7, 2002, respectively.   Signed by the Mayor on June 3, 2002, it was assigned Act No. 14-380 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.  D.C. Law 14-194 became effective on October 17, 2002. DC CODE § 22-3151 Current through December 11, 2012
