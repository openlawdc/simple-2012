The DC Code: § 16-2343.04 No right to jury trial.

Index → 16 Particular Actions, Proceedings and Matters. (Refs & Annos)

The parties to an action to establish parentage are not entitled to a jury trial.

Historical and Statutory

Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 18-33 substituted "parentage" for "paternity".
