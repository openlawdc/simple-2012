The DC Code: § 7-1501.02 Penalties; prosecutions.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)
a
Any person violating any provision of this subchapter or any regulation made pursuant to this subchapter shall be fined not more than $5,000, or be imprisoned for not more than 6 months, or both.
b
Prosecution for violations of this subchapter and regulations made pursuant to this subchapter shall be brought in the name of the District of Columbia upon information by the Corporation Counsel.
