The DC Code: § 26-504 Repeal of District of Columbia Credit Unions Act.

Index → 26 Banks and Other Financial Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

Effective 30 days after August 1, 1964, the District of Columbia Credit Unions Act (47 Stat. 326), as amended, is repealed and all organization certificates issued thereunder and still in force are revoked.
