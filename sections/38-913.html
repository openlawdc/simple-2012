The DC Code: § 38-913 Subsistence and transportation of children with disabilities.

Index → 38 Educational Institutions. (Refs & Annos)

The Board of Education is authorized to provide for the furnishing of subsistence supplies and transportation for children with severe disabilities attending special education schools or classes established for their benefit in the public school system of the District of Columbia.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 31-2213. 1973 Ed., § 31-1120. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 16-305, in the section heading, substituted "children with disabilities" for "handicapped children"; and substituted "children with severe disabilities" for "severely handicapped children".
