§ 25-410 Application for manager's license.

The application for a manager's license shall include:

1
Certification that he or she has obtained read a copy of this title;
2
Written statements or evidence establishing to the satisfaction of the Board that the applicant meets all of the qualifications set forth in § 25- 301; and
3
A copy of the applicant's alcohol training and education certificate.
