§ 29-408.20 Amendment by board of directors or members.

a
Except as otherwise provided in the articles of incorporation or bylaws, the members of a membership corporation may amend or repeal the corporation's bylaws.
b
The board of directors of a membership corporation or nonmembership corporation may amend or repeal the corporation's bylaws, unless the articles of incorporation or bylaws or § 29-408.21 or § 29-408.22 reserve that power exclusively to the members or a designated body in whole or part.
