§ 29-308.20 Amendment by board of directors or shareholders.

a
A corporation's shareholders may amend or repeal the corporation's bylaws.
b
A corporation's board of directors may amend or repeal the corporation's bylaws, unless:
1
The articles of incorporation, § 29-308.21 or, if applicable, § 29- 308.22 reserve that power exclusively to the shareholders in whole or part; or
2
The shareholders in amending, repealing, or adopting a bylaw expressly provide that the board of directors shall not amend, repeal, or reinstate that bylaw.
