§ 47-4005 Rules.

a
The Mayor shall, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.
b
The rules shall include standards for:
1
The transfer of funds to the Fund; and
2
The reimbursement of costs incurred by the Mayor in the collection, processing, accounting, or disbursement of the funds generated by the tax check-off.
