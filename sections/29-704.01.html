The DC Code: § 29-704.01 Becoming general partner.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)

A person becomes a general partner:

1
As provided in the partnership agreement:
2
Under § 29-708.01(3)(B) following the dissociation of a limited partnership's last general partner;
3
As the result of a merger under subchapter X of this chapter or a transaction under Chapter 2 of this title; or
4
With the consent of all the partners.

Historical and Statutory

Legislative History of Laws For history of Law 18-378, see notes under § 29-101.01. Uniform Law: This section is based on § 401 of the Uniform Limited Partnership Act (2001 Act).
