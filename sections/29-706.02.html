The DC Code: § 29-706.02 Effect of dissociation as limited partner.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
Upon a person's dissociation as a limited partner:
1
Subject to § 29-707.04, the person shall not have further rights as a limited partner;
2
The person's obligation of good faith and fair dealing as a limited partner under § 29-703.05(b) shall continue only as to matters arising and events occurring before the dissociation; and
3
Subject to § 29-707.04 and subchapter X of this chapter, any transferable interest owned by the person in the person's capacity as a limited partner immediately before dissociation shall be owned by the person as a mere transferee.
b
A person's dissociation as a limited partner shall not of itself discharge the person from any obligation to the limited partnership or the other partners which the person incurred while a limited partner.
