The DC Code: § 10-505.05 Transfer of member of Metropolitan Police to Capitol Police-- Definitions.

Index → 10 Parks, Public Buildings, Grounds, and Space. (Refs & Annos)

As used in this part:

1
The term "Metropolitan Police force" means the Metropolitan Police force of the District of Columbia.
2
The term "police service" means creditable service under § 5-704.
