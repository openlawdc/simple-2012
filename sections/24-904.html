The DC Code: § 24-904 Conditional release; unconditional discharge.

Index → 24 Prisoners and Their Treatment. (Refs & Annos)
a
A committed youth offender may be released conditionally under supervision whenever appropriate.
b
A committed youth offender may be unconditionally discharged at the end of 1 year from the date of conditional release.
c
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, subsections (a) and (b) of this section shall not apply to a youth offender convicted of any offense committed on or after August 5, 2000.
