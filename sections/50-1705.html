The DC Code: § 50-1705 Application of chapter.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)
a
This chapter shall apply to any person operating a motor vehicle in the District of Columbia.
b
This chapter shall apply to any motor vehicle that is used for personal use.
