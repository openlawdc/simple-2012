§ 2-1515.07 Rules; authority to execute contracts and grants.

a
The Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of this subchapter.
b
The Mayor may execute contracts, grants, and other legally binding documents to implement the provisions of this subchapter.
