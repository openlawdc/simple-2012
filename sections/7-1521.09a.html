The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on February 6, 1996, and March 5, 1996, respectively. Signed by the mayor on March 15, 1996, it was assigned Act No. 11-232 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 11-118 became effective on May 24, 1996.
