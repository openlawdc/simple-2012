The DC Code: § 50-1505.02 Interstate and intrastate privileges.

Index → 50 Motor and Non-Motor Vehicles and Traffic. (Refs & Annos)

Rental fleets and utility trailers, owned by any person or firm engaging in the business of renting such vehicle, shall be extended full interstate and intrastate privileges, provided the following:

1
Such vehicle or trailers are part of a rental fleet and are identifiable as being a part of such fleet; and
2
Such person or firm registers a portion of said vehicles or trailers as provided in § 50-1505.03.
