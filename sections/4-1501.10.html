The DC Code: § 4-1501.10 Penalties for disclosing confidential information.

Index → 4 Public Care Systems. (Refs & Annos)
a
An individual who discloses confidential information in violation of § 4-1501.08 is guilty of a criminal offense and, upon conviction, shall be fined not more than $1,000 or imprisoned for not more than 180 days, or both.
b
Prosecutions for violations of this chapter shall be brought in the Superior Court of the District of Colombia by the Office of the Attorney General.
