The DC Code: § 29-1002.02 Signing and filing of records pursuant to judicial order.

Index → 29 Business Organizations. (Refs & Annos)
a
If a person required by this chapter to sign or deliver a record to the Mayor for filing does not do so, the Superior Court, upon petition of an aggrieved person, may order:
1
The person to sign the record and deliver it to the Mayor for filing; or
2
Delivery of the unsigned record to the Mayor for filing.
b
An aggrieved person under subsection (a) of this section, other than the limited cooperative association or foreign cooperative to which the record pertains, shall make the association or foreign cooperative a party to the action brought to obtain the order.
c
An unsigned record filed pursuant to this section shall be effective.
