Law 12-268, the "Equal Opportunity for Local, Small, and Disadvantaged Business Enterprises Act of 1998," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 12- 616, which was referred to the Committee on Economic Development. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 1, 1998, and December 15, 1998, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on December 24, 1998, it was assigned Act No. 12-580 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 12-268 became effective on April 27, 1999.
