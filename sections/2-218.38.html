§ 2-218.38 Veteran-owned business enterprises.

A business enterprise shall be eligible for certification as a veteran-owned business enterprise if the business enterprise:

1
Meets the definition of a small business enterprise as described in § 2- 218.32;
2
Is not less than 51% owned and operated by one of more veterans (as defined in 38 U.S.C. § 101(2));
3
In the case of any publicly owned business, not less than 51% of the stock of which is owned by one or more veterans; and
4
One or more veterans control the management and daily operations.
