§ 38-2806 Accountability Plan.

The Superintendent shall, by December 15, 1998, provide an Accountability Plan for each school. The Accountability Plan shall set forth five-year student achievement test goals and year one benchmarks, as measured against a baseline derived from student performance levels determined by the "Stanford 9" tests administered in Spring 1998.
