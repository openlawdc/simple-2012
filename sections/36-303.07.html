§ 36-303.07 Application of subchapter.

a
This subchapter shall only apply to that portion of a marketing agreement which concerns the operation of a retail service station which is located within the District of Columbia and only to the extent of the business conducted by a retail dealer within the District of Columbia.
b
This subchapter shall apply to any and all marketing agreements entered into after April 19, 1977. The term "entered into" shall include any renewal, extension, modification, amendment, or novation of a preexisting marketing agreement.
c
This subchapter shall also apply to any failure to renew a preexisting marketing agreement.
