§ 26-1213 Federal exclusion.

This chapter shall not be construed to:

1
Grant the Superintendent or any other District agency jurisdiction over a federal association or any merger or consolidation of a federal association in which an association chartered by the District is not a party; or
2
Subject any federal association to any law or rule of the District or its agencies to which it is not otherwise subject.
