The DC Code: § 44-110.04 General building interior

Index → 44 Charitable and Curative Institutions. (Refs & Annos)
a
An ALR shall ensure that the interior of its facility including walls, ceilings, doors, windows, equipment, and fixtures are maintained structurally sound, sanitary, and in good repair.
b
An ALR shall ensure that floors and stairways provide a clean, slip-resistant, and safe surface, free of tripping hazards.
c
An ALR shall install and maintain assist handrails or grab bars, whenever practicable, on each side of interior stairways and on one side of corridors and in bathrooms.
d
An ALR shall provide common areas for social and recreational use totaling at least 35 square feet per resident for living, dining, therapy, and recreational activities.
