§ 32-1102 Applicability to employment in workplaces.

a
Except as otherwise provided in this section, this chapter shall apply to employment performed in any workplace in the District.
b
This chapter shall not apply to the premises of an establishment of the United States government unless the physical premises are controlled by an independent contractor.
c
Nothing in this chapter shall apply to working conditions of employees whose occupational safety and health is protected by the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 (42 U.S.C. § 2011 et seq.), or with respect to which the United States government exercises exclusive jurisdiction for purposes of occupational safety and health.
