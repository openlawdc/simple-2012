§ 35-253 Penalties.

Violation of § 35-251(b) shall be punishable by a fine of not less than $10 nor more than $50 for a 1st offense and by a fine of not less than $50 nor more than $100 or by imprisonment for not more than 10 days or both for each 2nd or subsequent offense. A violation of § 35-251(c) or (d) shall be punishable by a fine of not more than $300, imprisonment of not more than 90 days, not fewer than 30 hours of community service, or a combination of any 2 penalties, except that imprisonment and community service shall not be imposed together. A violation of § 35-216 shall be punishable by a fine of not more than $300, by imprisonment for not more than 10 days, or both.   All prosecutions under §§ 35-216 and 35-251 to 35-253 shall be brought by the Corporation Counsel.
