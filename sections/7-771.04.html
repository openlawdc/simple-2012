The DC Code: § 7-771.04 Appointment of Director.

Index → 7 Human Health Care and Safety. (Refs & Annos)

The Department shall be headed by a Director, who shall:

1
Be appointed by the Mayor with the advice and consent of the Council, pursuant to § 1-523.01(a);
2
Be qualified by experience and training to carry out the purposes of the Department as set forth in § 7-771.03; and
3
Serve at the pleasure of the Mayor.
