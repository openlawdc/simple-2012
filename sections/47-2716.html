The DC Code: § 47-2716 Denial of medical or mental health services for inability to pay prohibited.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)

No person shall be denied the services enumerated in §§ 47-2713 to 47- 2716 because of his or her inability to pay for those services.
