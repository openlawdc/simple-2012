The DC Code: § 22-3007 Defense to sexual abuse.

Index → 22 Criminal Offenses and Penalties. (Refs & Annos)

Consent by the victim is a defense to a prosecution under §§ 22-3002 to 22-3006, prosecuted alone or in conjunction with charges under § 22-3018 or §§ 22-401 and 22-403.

Historical and Statutory

Prior Codifications 1981 Ed., § 22-4107. Effect of Amendments D.C. Law 18-88 deleted "defense, which the defendant must establish by a preponderance of the evidence," following "defense".
