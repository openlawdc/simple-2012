§ 4-1305.04 Payment of processing fees and costs.

a
A person who is required to have a criminal records check under this part shall pay for:
1
The mandatory processing fee required by the Federal Bureau of Investigation for conducting the national criminal records check;
2
Reasonable administrative costs to the police for accessing the District criminal records history; and
3
Reasonable administrative costs to the Agency.
b
The Agency or a licensed child-placing agency may pay for processing fees and costs.
