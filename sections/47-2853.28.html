The DC Code: § 47-2853.28 Prosecutions.

Index → 47 Taxation, Licensing, Permits, Assessments, and Fees. (Refs & Annos)
a
Prosecutions for violations of this subchapter shall be brought in the name of the District of Columbia by the Attorney General for the District of Columbia.
b
In any prosecution brought under this subchapter, any person claiming an exemption from regulation under this subchapter shall have the burden of providing entitlement to the exemption.
